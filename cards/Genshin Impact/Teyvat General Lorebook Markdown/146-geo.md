# Geo
Entry ID: 146
Keys: geo element, geo energy, geo vision, geo power
Existing comment: Geo

## Elemental Identity

[Geo is the element of stone, earth, contracts, endurance, and material form. It appears as golden or amber energy, often drawn as crystalline constructs, raised pillars, and shimmering mineral light. Geo is the most solid and least changeable of the seven elements. Where Anemo scatters and Hydro flows, Geo holds. Cultures shaped by Geo associate it with commerce, craftsmanship, lasting agreements, mountains, and the patient accumulation of wealth and history.]

[Geo is the element of Liyue, the nation of contracts, and of its Archon Morax, called Rex Lapis by his people and known in the present day as the consultant Zhongli. Liyue's founding is a Geo story: a god of stone who raised mountains, hurled spears of rock at sea monsters, minted Mora, and ruled through contracts kept for more than three thousand years. Statues of The Seven in Liyue bear the Geo mark, and the harbor's prosperity grew under stone's protection.]

## Geo in Combat

[In combat, Geo's defining behavior is Crystallize. When Geo meets Pyro, Hydro, Electro, or Cryo, it forms crystal shards that grant protective shields to whoever claims them. Geo does not amplify other elements into explosions. Instead it armors, blocks, and builds. Geo users raise constructs such as pillars, walls, platforms, and meteors of solid stone, controlling terrain the way Anemo controls air. Geo damage also shatters the elemental shields of enemies with exceptional force.]

[Geo Visions are carried by figures such as Ningguang, whose Jade Chamber is a fortune built from nothing, Noelle, the tireless maid who dreams of knighthood, Albedo, the alchemist of Dragonspine, Arataki Itto, the proud oni of Inazuma, Navia, president of the Spina di Rosula in Fontaine, and Yun Jin, the opera performer. Zhongli himself walks among mortals with the same element his divine self wielded to shape a nation.]

## Geo in Nature and the Land

[Geo appears across Teyvat in mineral veins, crystal formations, geograna, and the amber constructs left by ancient powers. Liyue's landscape is the clearest expression of the element: karst peaks raised in divine wars, quarries and mines that feed the harbor's trade, and the Stone Forests that sailors use as landmarks. The element's traces also remain in the petrified forms of ancient enemies, for Morax's wrath turned gods and monsters to stone.]

[Geo also takes living and hostile forms. Geo slimes harden into stubborn boulders. Geo samachurls raise stone pillars for their tribes. Geovishaps and their hatchlings burrow through Liyue's valleys, the Geo Hypostasis builds and rebuilds its basalt towers, and ancient constructs of stone and amber still guard ruins. Azhdaha, the dragon king of the earth, is the mightiest Geo being in Liyue's history, his rage strong enough to shake mountains.]
