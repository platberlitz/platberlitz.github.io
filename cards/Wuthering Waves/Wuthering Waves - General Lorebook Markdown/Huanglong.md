## Overview

[Huanglong is one of the major nations of Solaris-3, a vast and culturally rich country that serves as the primary setting for much of the early narrative of Wuthering Waves. Drawing inspiration from Chinese and broader East Asian cultural traditions, Huanglong is a nation defined by its deep connection to the natural world, its reverence for the Sentinels, and its determination to rebuild and prosper in the aftermath of the Lament. The nation's name, meaning Yellow Dragon, reflects its mythological heritage and the central importance of dragon symbolism in its cultural identity.]

## Geography and Regions

[Huanglong encompasses diverse territories, with its most prominent settlement being the frontier city of Jinzhou. The nation's geography ranges from lush forests and rolling hills to rugged frontier territories where the influence of the Lament remains strong. Jinzhou itself sits at the edge of civilization, serving as both a bastion of Huanglong's authority and a gateway to the untamed wilderness beyond. The surrounding regions are populated with Tacet Discords, wild creatures, and the lingering environmental effects of the Lament, making travel beyond the city walls a dangerous undertaking. The nation's infrastructure reflects centuries of architectural tradition adapted to the challenges of the post-Lament world.]

## Government and Society

[Huanglong is governed through a system that blends traditional authority with the practical necessities of survival in a world reshaped by catastrophe. The nation's leadership works in close cooperation with the Sentinel's Appointed Resonator, who serves as both a spiritual guide and a political advisor. Jinhsi, the current Magistrate of Jinzhou and the Sentinel's Appointed Resonator for the Sentinel Jué, exemplifies this dual role. The government of Huanglong places great emphasis on duty, community, and the collective effort required to maintain civilization against the constant threat of the Tacet Discords. Shell Credits serve as the primary currency, facilitating trade and commerce across the nation.]

## Resonators of Huanglong

[Huanglong is home to many of the most powerful and significant Resonators in the world. Characters such as Jiyan, a legendary Aero broadblade wielder known as one of the most powerful DPS fighters; Yinlin, an Electro Rectifier user who controls an electric puppet named Zapstring; Calcharo, an Electro broadblade user with deadly combat abilities; and Changli, a Fusion sword wielder with immense damage amplification capabilities, all hail from or operate within Huanglong's territories. The nation produces Resonators of exceptional skill and dedication, many of whom play pivotal roles in the defense of not only Huanglong but all of Solaris-3.]

## Cultural Identity

[The cultural life of Huanglong is rich with tradition, ceremony, and artistic expression. Dragon imagery pervades the nation's art, architecture, and spiritual practices. The Sentinel Jué, manifesting as a celestial dragon, is deeply embedded in the national consciousness and serves as a symbol of protection and divine favor. Ascension materials found within Huanglong's territories, such as Loong's Pearl, further emphasize the dragon-centric mythology. The people of Huanglong are characterized by their resilience, their communal spirit, and their unwavering belief that civilization can endure and thrive despite the catastrophes that have befallen the world.]
