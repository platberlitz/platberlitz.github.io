Existing comment: Jinhsi and Rover
SELECTIVE

[Jinhsi views Rover as someone who shares the inner shape of resolve. Her words do not begin with Rover's mystery, lost memories, shifting elemental expression, or the strange way their arrival catalyzes events across Solaris-3. Instead, Jinhsi says she can feel in her heart that both of them have something they will fiercely defend, a cause worth fighting for. This is a deeply personal recognition from a Magistrate whose life has been defined by public duty. Jinhsi does not need Rover's full past to sense the direction of their will. She recognizes a fellow defender before she can fully explain what history made them so.]

[The comparison is not symmetrical biography, but Jinhsi's attempt to name kinship across uncertainty. She is Sentinel Jué's appointed Resonator, the young guardian of Jinzhou, and a ruler who has pledged herself to defending her city and countrymen to her last breath. Rover is a wanderer, a traveler without clear memory, moving through dangerous landscapes and repeatedly drawn into conflicts that decide more than personal survival. Jinhsi's heart reads through the missing information. Whatever Rover has forgotten, they act like someone pulled by a cause that refuses to let them stand aside.]

[Her confidence that neither of them will back down, no matter what it takes, reveals both admiration and burden. Jinhsi values perseverance because Jinzhou needs leaders and allies who do not collapse when the Lament's legacy presses against hope. Yet the phrase no matter what it takes also carries the danger of self-sacrifice. Jinhsi knows the cost of fiercely defending something larger than oneself, and she senses that Rover may know it too, even if their memories remain incomplete. The relationship therefore rests on mutual seriousness as perceived by Jinhsi. Rover is not merely a useful outsider or a narrative catalyst. To her, Rover is someone whose presence confirms that there are others willing to stand before impossible futures and choose protection anyway.]
