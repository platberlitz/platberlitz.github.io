## Overview and Goals

[Fractsidus is an extremist organization dedicated to bringing about the True Lament, a prophesied catastrophic frequency event that its members believe will transcend the current age and herald a new reality. Unlike ordinary criminal or insurgent groups, Fractsidus operates on an eschatological framework: its members genuinely believe that the destruction they seek is not an end but a threshold, a necessary passage through which civilization must travel. The organization venerates Threnodians as agents of this transformation and views existing governments and defensive institutions as obstacles delaying an inevitable, even desirable, cosmic process.]

## Leadership and Key Figures

[Phrolova serves as a Fractsidus Overseer and directs much of the organization's operational activity. Described as an uncanny, deadly conductor who walks the fine line between life and death, Phrolova can attune the very frequencies of being with a silent wave of her baton, conducting symphonies of souls that can sculpt a better world or summon legions to wreak havoc. Under her direction, Fractsidus deploys agents including Scar, a dangerous operative with formidable combat abilities, and Schwarzloch, whose activities suggest a role in logistics or infiltration. The organization's broader leadership structure remains obscured, with indications that figures operating beyond Phrolova's level set strategic direction.]

## Operations and Tactics

[Fractsidus conducts operations across multiple regions, targeting Resonator institutions, Lament-monitoring infrastructure, and civilian populations in patterns designed to destabilize and provoke. The organization has been linked to deliberate Lament-provocation attempts, Tacet Discord manipulation, and attacks on Black Shores assets including Tethys System terminals. Mortefi, a Resonator driven by personal loss, has directed his rage at Fractsidus agents, pursuing them with single-minded fury. The organization's tactics favor psychological warfare, frequency contamination, and the exploitation of existing conflicts to weaken its enemies from within before striking openly.]

## Ideology and Recruitment

[Fractsidus recruits from populations traumatized by Lament events, Resonators who have been marginalized or persecuted, and individuals who find the True Lament doctrine compelling for philosophical or emotional reasons. The organization presents itself as offering meaning in the face of cosmic horror, framing the senseless destruction of Laments as purposeful steps toward transcendence. This ideology makes its members extraordinarily resilient under interrogation, as they genuinely believe their suffering serves a higher cause.]
