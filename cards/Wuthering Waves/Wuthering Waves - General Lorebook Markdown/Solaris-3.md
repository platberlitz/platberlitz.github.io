## Overview

[Solaris-3, frequently abbreviated as SOL3 in both military and civilian parlance, is the primary setting of Wuthering Waves and the world upon which all known events of the game take place. It is a planet orbiting the star Solaris, from which it derives its designation as the third body in the Solisylum stellar system. The planet is home to a rich tapestry of civilizations, ecosystems, and cultures that have developed over untold ages, only to be irrevocably reshaped by the catastrophic event known as the Lament.]

## Geography and Regions

[The surface of Solaris-3 is remarkably diverse, encompassing a vast array of biomes and terrains that range from temperate grasslands and dense forests to frozen wastelands, volcanic zones, and ancient architectural ruins. Among the most prominent known regions are the Central Plains, which serve as a heartland of human activity; Jinzhou, a fortified city-state governed by a Magistrate and protected by the Sentinel's Appointed Resonator; and the Desorock Highlands, a rugged frontier territory. Other significant areas include the Dimmr Plains, an expansive terrain marked by Void Storm contamination; the Roya Frostlands, a frozen expanse in the planet's northern latitudes where Void Storm activity is particularly intense; the Sanguis Plateaus, elevated terrain with its own unique ecology; and the Chronorift Metropolis, an urban center characterized by temporal anomalies. Subterranean and submerged regions are equally prominent, with zones such as Tethys Deep, the Vault Underground, and the Fabricatorium of the Deep representing vast underground and aquatic environments that harbor their own dangers and secrets.]

[Additional territories include Rinascita, a region shaped by the cultural legacy of its inhabitants; Septimont, a mountainous zone; Lahai-Roi, a distinctive landscape dotted with resonance infrastructure; Beohr Waters and the Riccioli Islands, maritime and archipelago regions; Avinoleum, a zone with its own unique material composition; and the Solvein Canopy area, noted for its dense arboreal cover. The sheer geographic scope of Solaris-3 reflects a world of immense scale, one in which travel between regions demands considerable effort and where each territory has developed its own identity in response to the pressures of the post-Lament era.]

## Civilization and Society

[The civilizations of Solaris-3 are defined by their relationship to Resonance, a fundamental force of the world that certain individuals, known as Resonators, can perceive and harness. Resonators wield one of six elements: Glacio (ice), Fusion (fire), Electro (thunder), Aero (wind), Spectro (light), and Havoc (darkness). These gifted individuals form the backbone of military defense, governance, and exploration across the planet. Settlements range from fortified city-states like Jinzhou, with its established political hierarchy and Sentinel traditions, to frontier outposts and mobile encampments. The existence of Tacet Fields, zones of concentrated Tacet Discord activity, has forced humanity to develop sophisticated strategies for survival, including the establishment of Resonance Beacons and Resonance Nexus networks that serve as waypoints and communication infrastructure across the wilderness.]

[Technology on Solaris-3 blends advanced engineering with resonance-based principles. Equipment such as the Expedition Motorbike, Mechascout drones, Projector devices, and holographic systems indicate a civilization with considerable scientific achievement, even as it struggles against the existential threats posed by the Lament's aftermath. The planet also hosts hostile factions such as the Exiles and the Fractsidus, organized groups that oppose or exist outside established societal structures. Despite these hardships, the people of Solaris-3 continue to pursue knowledge, art, and community, as evidenced by the existence of cultural landmarks, the Archive of Melodies, and ongoing survey and restoration projects aimed at reclaiming territory lost to the Lament's corruption.]

## The World After the Lament

[Solaris-3 in the present era is a world defined by recovery and resilience. The Lament shattered the planet's former stability, unleashing Void Storms that distort reality itself and spawning the Tacet Discords that now roam the land. Entire regions remain contaminated by residual Voidmatter or locked in perpetual Lament Residue Zones where Tacet Discords affected by Irideglow contamination grow increasingly dangerous. The concept of SOL3 Phase Ascension, a system by which the ambient danger level and reward potential of the world fluctuates, reflects the precarious balance that civilization maintains against encroaching chaos. Yet Solaris-3 endures. Its Resonators push back the boundaries of the unknown, its cities rebuild and expand, and its people look to the stars of the Solisylum with a mixture of trepidation and hope, determined to reclaim their world from the shadow cast by the Lament.]
