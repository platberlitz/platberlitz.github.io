## The Jinzhou Worker

[Taoqi is a worker from Jinzhou, the bustling port city that serves as the economic and cultural hub of the Huanglong region. Unlike the Resonators, fighters, and institutional agents who dominate most lorebook entries, Taoqi represents the vast majority of people in the world, those whose contributions to society are measured not in spectacular displays of elemental power or heroic individual combat but in the daily labor that keeps cities functioning, infrastructure maintained, and communities supplied with the material necessities of life. Her connection to Jinzhou's infrastructure, defense, and daily operations places her in a position of quiet but essential importance: she is one of the people without whom the city simply could not operate, however little attention or recognition her work receives from those who benefit from it.]

## Infrastructure and Defense

[Taoqi's involvement in both infrastructure and defense reflects the reality that in a world beset by Tacet Discord events, Fractsidus operations, and the ongoing consequences of the Lament, the distinction between civilian infrastructure work and military preparedness has become increasingly blurred. The systems that deliver clean water, maintain transportation networks, and keep buildings standing are the same systems that must be resilient against attack, sabotage, and the environmental disruptions that Resonance-related events can produce. Taoqi's work presumably encompasses the maintenance, repair, and fortification of the physical structures that Jinzhou depends upon, a role that requires technical skill, physical endurance, and an understanding of how systems interconnect that is at least as sophisticated as the combat knowledge possessed by dedicated fighters. Her Havoc Broadblade, in this context, is not merely a weapon but a tool, something used with equal facility for construction, demolition, and self-defense when circumstances require.]

## The Defensive Scaling

[Taoqi's combat mechanics in the game reflect her identity as a worker rather than a warrior, she scales with defense rather than attack, making her effective precisely because she can endure rather than because she can dominate. Her Rock Steady defensive state reduces incoming damage by 35% and can trigger counterattacks that scale with her defensive stats, reflecting a fighting philosophy centered on resilience, preparation, and the strategic use of fortification rather than aggressive offense. Her resonance shields provide fixed damage reduction and can be consumed to fuel her Forte gauge, a mechanic that mirrors the real-world principle that defensive structures can be repurposed as offensive resources when circumstances demand. Taoqi embodies the truth that the most important battles are not always fought by those who seek them, and that the people who build and maintain the world are often those best equipped to defend it when the need arises.]
