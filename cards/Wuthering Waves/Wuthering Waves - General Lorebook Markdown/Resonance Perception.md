## Sensory Mechanism

[Resonance Perception is the innate sensory faculty that allows a Resonator to detect, interpret, and respond to the frequency waves that permeate the world of Solaris-3. Unlike ordinary human senses, which are limited to visible light, audible sound, and physical touch, Resonance Perception operates on a fundamentally different plane, enabling a Resonator to perceive the vibrational signatures of objects, creatures, and environments that would otherwise remain invisible and unknowable. This ability functions as both a passive awareness and an active investigative tool, making it indispensable for navigation, combat, and exploration across the dangerous and altered landscapes of the post-Lament world.]

## Environmental Detection

[One of the primary applications of Resonance Perception is the ability to detect hidden or obscured environmental information. Resonators can sense the presence of Resonance Nexuses, which store critical data about surrounding areas, and Sonance Caskets, which contain sealed artifacts activated by sonic resonance. This perceptual faculty also enables the detection of frequency signals embedded in the environment, such as the signals encountered during Capture Frequency Signal and Transmit Frequency Signal puzzles. A Resonator must attune their perception to decode these signals, tapping or holding in precise rhythm to match the incoming waveform, a process that demands both sensitivity and concentration.]

## Combat Applications

[In combat, Resonance Perception is equally vital. It allows a Resonator to sense the approach of attacks, gauge the elemental frequencies emitted by Tacet Discords, and identify openings for counterattacks and dodges. The timing-dependent mechanics of combat, including perfect dodges, counterattacks, and the accumulation of Concerto Energy, all rely on a Resonator's ability to perceive and react to the shifting frequency landscape in real time. A Resonator with heightened Resonance Perception can anticipate enemy movements, detect weak points, and synchronize their abilities with allies to maximize team effectiveness through the Intro and Outro skill system.]

## Variability and Training

[The acuity of Resonance Perception varies significantly among individuals. Some Resonators possess naturally heightened perceptual sensitivity, while others must train extensively to refine their abilities. Factors such as elemental affinity, emotional state, and physical condition can all influence the clarity and range of perception. Resonators who specialize in Spectro elements, for example, often exhibit particularly acute perceptual abilities due to their alignment with light and information-based frequencies. Training regimens at Resonator academies and field exercises in hostile territory are designed to push perceptual limits, teaching Resonators to distinguish subtle frequency variations and react to them instinctively under pressure.]

## Connection to Frequency Waves

[Resonance Perception is ultimately a manifestation of the same fundamental resonance that defines all Resonator abilities. It is the sensory dimension of the Resonator's connection to the frequency waves that saturate Solaris-3, a connection that also enables their combat powers, their ability to absorb Echoes, and their capacity to interact with resonant technology. Without Resonance Perception, a Resonator would be effectively blind to the forces that shape their world, reduced to reacting only to what ordinary senses can detect. This perceptual faculty is therefore not merely an auxiliary skill but a core component of what makes a Resonator capable of surviving and thriving in the altered reality of the post-Lament era.]
