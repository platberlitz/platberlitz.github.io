[The term "Resonators" in the plural often refers collectively to the global population of awakened individuals rather than the abstract concept. Across the city-states and frontier settlements of Solaris-3, Resonators form a vital but sometimes feared minority. Organizations such as the Black Shores, the Huaxu Academy, and various regional militias maintain registries of known Resonators, tracking their Forte classifications and Rabelle's Curve readings. In Jinzhou, Resonators serve openly in the Midnight Rangers and the civilian governance. In Ragunna and the Rinascita city-states, Resonators occupy more complex roles, entangled with religious authority, gladiatorial tradition, and political intrigue. The Fractsidus, by contrast, views Resonators as instruments or catalysts for the next great Lament.]
