# Howler

## Definition

[Howler-type Tacet Discords mimic flora or fauna. The source entry defines them as frequency-born entities that resemble beasts, plants, or natural creatures while remaining Tacet Discords. Their natural appearance is therefore a surface language rather than proof of natural origin. A Howler may look like something that belongs in an ecosystem, but its body is a Reverberation Body sustained by chaotic frequency after the Lament. It imitates life without being ordinary life.]

## Flora and Fauna

[The category covers both plantlike and animal-like reference points. This range matters because it keeps Howlers from being reduced to beasts alone. A Howler may evoke a predator, a grazing animal, a flying creature, a plant form, or another natural organism, but the common thread is mimicry of living nature. That sets Howlers apart from Whisperin and Murmurin, which point toward human traces, from Tranquilites, which point toward inorganic natural matter, and from Staticoids, which point toward man-made objects.]

## Frequency-Born Nature

[Because Howlers are Tacet Discords, their bodies and behavior should remain anchored in frequency. Their resemblance to flora or fauna may influence how they move, threaten, hide, or occupy terrain, but it should not erase their post-Lament origin. They possess the same broad Tacet Discord nature: chaotic frequency formation, a Reverberation Body, consumption of frequencies, and the possibility of leaving Echo traces after decay. A Howler is not a wolf, flower, vine, bird, or beast in the normal sense. It is an imitation shaped by unstable resonance.]

## Dossier Use

[For lorebook use, Howler is the correct label when a Tacet Discord's mimicry is directed toward the living natural world outside specifically human social form. It can support wilderness danger, corrupted ecology, field research, and scenes where familiar life becomes unreliable. A source-aligned dossier should emphasize the contrast between natural appearance and frequency-born reality. The more convincing the plant or animal resemblance becomes, the more important it is to remember that the entity belongs to the Tacet Discord phenomenon created in the conditions after the Lament.]
