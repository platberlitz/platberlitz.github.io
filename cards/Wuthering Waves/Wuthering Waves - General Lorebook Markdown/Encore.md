# Encore

## Definition

[Encore is a Black Shores girl with boundless energy, imagination, affection for stories, easy friendship, and an adventurous view of the world. The source entry defines her through brightness and movement rather than through cynicism or guardedness. She approaches the world as a place where stories can be made, friendships can appear quickly, and unfamiliar places can become exciting instead of only threatening. Her Black Shores connection gives that warmth a setting context, but it does not erase her childlike openness.]

## Appearance

[The source describes Encore as a small pink-haired girl in a black, white, and pink outfit, usually framed by her black and white Wooly plush companions. This makes her look playful and storybook-bright. The contrast is important: she belongs to the Black Shores, an organization often associated with mystery and serious work, yet her visual presence is soft, theatrical, and full of companionship. The Woolies are part of how she is read at a glance, turning her silhouette into something closer to a moving tale.]

## Social Energy

## Dossier Use

[A source-aligned Encore dossier should preserve both her innocence and her capability. She is affectionate, imaginative, energetic, adventurous, and socially magnetic, but not empty comic relief. Her stories and Wooly companions shape how she relates to people and places.
