## Operational Mandate

[The Internal Security Agency is a governmental security organization operating across the territories of Mingting and Chongzhou, two major population centers on Solaris-3. Its mandate encompasses the maintenance of internal order, counterintelligence, threat assessment, and the protection of state interests against both domestic and external dangers. The Agency operates in the complex space between conventional law enforcement and military intelligence, handling cases that fall outside the jurisdiction of regular police forces and below the threshold that would trigger full military mobilization. Its reach extends into monitoring Resonator activity, investigating Fractsidus operations, and managing the security implications of Tacet Discord incursions into populated areas.]

## Leadership and Qiuyuan

[Qiuyuan, a five-star Aero sword wielder, leads a significant portion of the Agency's operational work. His hybrid role as both sub-DPS combatant and support specialist reflects the Agency's own hybrid nature, functioning as both a fighting force and an intelligence apparatus. Qiuyuan's management of his unique Swordster's Soliloquy resource and his ability to enter the Inkplash of Mind stance suggest specialized combat training that goes beyond standard Agency preparation, indicating he may have brought exceptional personal skill to his leadership position. His enhancement of team Echo Skill damage through Bamboo's Shade and his ability to grant allies Crit Damage bonuses suggest a commanding style that elevates those under his direction rather than merely directing them.]

## Regional Security Landscape

[The areas of Mingting and Chongzhou present distinct security challenges that shape the Agency's operations. These regions contain dense populations, active resonance infrastructure, and proximity to territories where Tacet Discord activity is common. The Agency must balance the need for visible security presence with covert operations against organized threats like Fractsidus. Its personnel include both Resonators and non-Resonator operatives, though the most critical field assignments fall to those with resonance capabilities. The Agency's work is often unglamorous, involving surveillance, investigation, and prevention rather than the dramatic confrontations that define military engagements, but its effectiveness is measured in threats never realized and crises avoided.]
