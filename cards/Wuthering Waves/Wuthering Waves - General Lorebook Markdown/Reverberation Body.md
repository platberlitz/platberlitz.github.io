# Reverberation Body

## Definition

[A Reverberation Body is the manifested form of a Tacet Discord. The source entry emphasizes manifestation, resemblance, and frequency rather than ordinary anatomy. These bodies can resemble animals, people, plants, objects, or stranger shapes depending on the frequencies they mimic. The name matters because it describes a body made meaningful by reverberation, not by inherited biology. A Tacet Discord is sustained by resonant frequency, and the Reverberation Body is the visible or encounterable expression of that unstable sustaining pattern.]

## Form and Mimicry

[The form of a Reverberation Body should not be confused with the origin of the being. A Tacet Discord may look humanlike, beastlike, plantlike, mineral, artificial, or otherwise strange, but those visual cues are imitations assembled through chaotic frequency. This explains why Tacet Discord classifications can be organized around what they mimic. Whisperin and Murmurin bodies point toward human traces. Howler bodies imitate flora or fauna. Tranquilite bodies echo inorganic natural matter. Staticoid bodies mimic or parasitize artificial objects. The Reverberation Body is the shared concept underneath those varied appearances.]

## Not Ordinary Biology

[The source states that the term reflects a Tacet Discord being sustained by resonant frequency rather than ordinary biology. That distinction should guide any dossier usage. A Reverberation Body may move like a creature, bear limbs, carry objectlike parts, or display plantlike growth, but its coherence comes from frequency. It can therefore violate expectations tied to muscles, organs, age, species, or manufacture. Its shape is a resonant expression, and damage to that expression is not the same as injury to a natural organism.]

## Lorebook Use

[For source-aligned writing, Reverberation Body is the correct term when describing the form a Tacet Discord presents in the world. It is useful for separating what is seen from what the entity is. A survivor may report a beast, a statue, a personlike figure, or a tool that moved by itself, but a researcher can identify the underlying structure as a Tacet Discord's Reverberation Body. This keeps scenes anchored in the setting's frequency language while allowing the outward shape to remain varied, uncanny, and dangerous.]
