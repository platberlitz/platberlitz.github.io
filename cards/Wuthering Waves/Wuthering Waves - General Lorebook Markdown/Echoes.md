# Echoes

## Definition

[Echoes are lingering frequency forms left after Tacet Discords or related phenomena decay. The source entry places them in the same ecology as Remnant Energy across Solaris-3. They are not ordinary trophies or inert remains. They are patterns that continue after the original manifestation has broken down, preserving a usable trace of frequency. This is why an Echo can matter after a Tacet Discord is gone: the being may decay, but the frequency shape it leaves behind can still be recognized, collected, studied, or used.]

## Resonator Use

[A Resonator can use an Echo to imitate an ability or draw on a stored frequency pattern. This makes Echoes practical as well as archival. They carry enough structure for a Resonator to call on something of the original trace without needing the original Tacet Discord to remain alive or intact. The source wording should be kept narrow: the Resonator draws on a stored pattern or imitates an ability. The Echo is therefore neither a full resurrected creature nor a generic spell, but a lingering frequency form with usable shape.]

## Relation to Remnant Energy

[Echoes belong to the ecology of Remnant Energy because both involve persistence after ordinary form has vanished. Remnant Energy is broader, including residue left by life, emotion, memory, and catastrophe in unstable locations, disaster sites, Sonoro Spheres, Echo traces, and Tacet Discord remains. Echoes are more specific, recognizable lingering forms that may be equipped or invoked by Resonators. The relationship is useful for dossier continuity: Remnant Energy describes the residue field, while Echoes describe particular preserved patterns inside that larger frequency ecology.]

## Dossier Use

[In lore writing, Echoes should carry the feeling of aftermath made functional. A battle, decay event, or related phenomenon may end, yet an Echo can still preserve its signature. Researchers might value it as evidence, Resonators might value it as a tool, and survivors might fear it as proof that the post-Lament world does not let dangerous frequencies disappear cleanly. Source-aligned Echo writing should avoid treating them as simple loot only. They are lingering frequency forms whose usefulness is inseparable from the instability that produced them.]
