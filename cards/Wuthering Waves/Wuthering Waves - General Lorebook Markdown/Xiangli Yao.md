# Xiangli Yao

## Identity

[Xiangli Yao is a Mutant-class Resonator aligned with the Electro frequency, a Gauntlets user, and the bearer of the title Matter Weaver. His origin and national context are both Huanglong, while his active public life is centered in Jinzhou through Huaxu Academy. The source identifies him as the Principal Investigator in charge of academics at Huaxu Academy's Jinzhou Campus, a role that makes him more than a gifted individual researcher. He is an institutional figure responsible for academic direction inside one of Huanglong's major scientific bodies. His identity therefore combines Resonator status, public scholarship, technical authority, and civic service. In a setting where Resonators are examined because their Fortes can affect bodies, minds, and surroundings, Xiangli Yao stands out as someone whose own abilities are tied directly to the mechanics and research culture he helps lead.]

[The record emphasizes his youth as much as his achievement. Xiangli Yao is described as Huaxu Academy's youngest multi-disciplinary scientist, and he is specifically named as an expert in Automata Mechanics. That distinction gives his identity a distinctive balance: he is young enough for his rise to seem exceptional, but his title and position already place him among those responsible for guiding serious research. Matter Weaver is not simply a combat epithet. It reflects the way his Resonator nature, prosthetic engineering, and scientific vocation converge. His gauntlet-based combat profile and his prosthetic arm both point toward a character whose body, tools, and scholarship are inseparable. He belongs to Huanglong by origin, Jinzhou by service, and Huaxu Academy by vocation.]

## Appearance

[Xiangli Yao is a slim young man of average height, with short light-brown hair tipped gray and piercing blue eyes ringed with pale pink. His design gives him a polished academic presence rather than an overtly martial silhouette, but the mechanical details prevent that calm surface from reading as ordinary. The most important physical feature in the source is his right arm, which has been replaced by a cybernetic prosthetic. The prosthetic is not an accessory or a detachable tool in the way a weapon might be. It is a defining part of his body, his visual identity, and his Resonator record, since it also bears his Tacet Mark.]

[His clothing reinforces the connection between refined intellect and engineered power. He wears a blue-and-white suit with patterned dark-blue panels, white coattails, and orange electrical accents. The palette fits an Electro Resonator while still preserving the neat, formal lines of someone associated with an academy rather than a battlefield unit. His outfit is also asymmetric, with one sleeveless side that displays the mechanical arm at the center of his design. That asymmetry matters because it refuses to hide the prosthetic. Xiangli Yao's appearance presents the arm as both vulnerability and achievement, a visible sign that his body has been altered and that he has integrated that alteration into his professional and Resonator identity.]

## Forte

[Xiangli Yao's associated Forte is Matter Weaver, documented under Resonance Assessment 2603-G. Post-Awakening observations showed that he can perform Metalmorph by manipulating the shape of his prosthetic arm and storing energy within it. This makes his Forte unusually bound to a specific physical medium. Rather than simply projecting Electro force outward, he channels transformation, stored power, and material reshaping through the prosthesis that has replaced his right arm. The result is a Forte profile that reads as technical, embodied, and materially precise. It matches his Academy role because the power itself behaves like a research subject: it involves structure, energy storage, unknown metal, and observable changes in a constructed limb.]

[The assessment also records a strong Syntony reaction, but the cause of his Awakening remains unclear. Investigators could not recover samples of the unknown metal substance involved, leaving a gap in the chain of evidence. That uncertainty is the reason the source identifies Xiangli Yao as a Mutant Resonator. His classification is therefore not a casual label. It reflects an Awakening whose mechanism could not be fully reconstructed, a Forte expressed through a cybernetic limb, and a material anomaly that resisted later analysis. Matter Weaver is powerful because it lets him reshape and energize metal through his prosthetic arm, but it is also scientifically provocative because the source preserves unanswered questions around the metal substance and the Awakening event.]

[His resonance stability profile is notably controlled. Xiangli Yao's waveform graph shows elliptical fluctuations, the Time Domain pattern is stable, and no abnormal waveforms are recorded. His frequency displays high stability, with minimal risk of Overclocking. This profile is important because many Resonator records treat power as inseparable from danger, instability, or a history of crisis. Xiangli Yao's case instead presents a high-functioning Resonator whose unusual body modification and Mutant classification do not translate into an unstable current condition. The unknown origin of the Awakening remains serious, but his present waveform data suggests disciplined regulation and a Forte that can be carried safely within ordinary activity, research work, and field use.]

## Personality

[The dedicated personality facet in the source is marked only as needing future addition, so Xiangli Yao's characterization must be drawn carefully from the other main entries rather than inflated beyond the record. The history entry describes him as a gentle yet rational soul, with unique insights and a clear commitment to Automata Mechanics. Those few words are dense. Gentle suggests that his brilliance does not express itself primarily as harshness or domination. Rational suggests that his decisions are guided by evidence, proportion, and careful thought. Unique insights explain why he can stand out even among Academy researchers, while commitment shows that his talent is sustained by discipline rather than mere natural advantage.]

[His personality is therefore best understood as calm intellectual seriousness joined to humane restraint. Xiangli Yao is young, highly accomplished, and responsible for academics at Huaxu Academy's Jinzhou Campus, but the record does not frame him as reckless or self-mythologizing. His stable resonance profile supports the impression of control, and his expertise in Automata Mechanics suggests patience with complex systems, repeated observation, and long technical work. The cybernetic prosthetic that carries his Tacet Mark could easily define him as a figure of spectacle, yet the main facets present him more as a researcher who has incorporated extraordinary circumstances into disciplined practice. He is exceptional, but the exceptional quality is quiet, methodical, and constructive.]

## History

[Xiangli Yao's known history is anchored in Huaxu Academy and in the unusual speed of his professional rise. The source names him as the Academy's Principal Investigator and repeats that he is in charge of academics at the Jinzhou Campus. It also calls him the Academy's youngest multi-disciplinary scientist, which makes his age a central element of his biography. He has reached a position of intellectual responsibility earlier than expected, and he has done so in a field environment where Resonator research, Automata Mechanics, Tacetite study, Tacetite Weaponry, safety engineering, and scientific investigation all matter to Huanglong's survival. His history is not recorded as a simple ascent through prestige. It is recorded as a life already tied to institutional duty.]

[The Awakening record forms the other major historical pillar. At some point after Awakening, observations revealed the Metalmorph ability of his prosthetic arm. The arm could change shape and store energy, and a strong Syntony reaction was observed. Yet the cause of the Awakening could not be determined, because samples of the unknown metal substance could not be recovered. This leaves Xiangli Yao's past with a deliberate unresolved core. The source can classify him as a Mutant Resonator, document Matter Weaver, describe his stable frequency, and identify the prosthetic arm as the bearer of his Tacet Mark, but it cannot fully explain the originating event. For a scientist, that missing evidence is especially significant. His own body and Forte contain a research problem that remains open.]

[The history facet also preserves a fragment labeled as an interview: years later, the speaker found themselves standing at that door again, and the Xiangli family had always been a star in Jinzhou's journalistic record before the text cuts off. Because the fragment is incomplete, it should not be treated as a finished biography. Its value is subtler. It suggests that Xiangli Yao's family name had public visibility in Jinzhou, enough to be remembered by journalism or public commentary, and it places his personal history within a broader civic memory. The fragment does not explain what happened at the door or why the family was notable, but it adds texture to the existing profile. Xiangli Yao is not only an Academy title and a Resonator assessment. He is also someone with a family background that the record begins to frame as publicly recognizable in Jinzhou.]

## Affiliations

[Xiangli Yao's affiliations are Jinzhou and Huaxu Academy, with Huanglong as both origin and nation. This affiliation pattern is straightforward but important. Jinzhou gives him a civic setting, a frontier city where scientific work has practical defensive and social consequences. Huaxu Academy gives him institutional identity, professional authority, and a field of research broad enough to contain both his Automata Mechanics expertise and his own Resonator case. Huanglong gives him national context, placing his work inside the wider cultural and political frame that supports the Academy.]

[Within Huaxu Academy, Xiangli Yao's role as Principal Investigator in charge of academics at the Jinzhou Campus makes him a central scholarly figure rather than a peripheral specialist. His expertise in Automata Mechanics suits an institution whose fields include Resonator research, Automata Mechanics, Tacetite study, Tacetite Weaponry, safety engineering, and scientific investigation. He stands at the intersection of these concerns: a Resonator whose body bears an unusual prosthetic-linked Tacet Mark, an Electro combatant whose Forte manipulates metal and stores energy, and a scientist whose work is formally embedded in the Academy. His affiliations show a person whose private anomaly has been converted into public expertise. Through Jinzhou and Huaxu Academy, Matter Weaver becomes not only Xiangli Yao's title, but also a way of describing his place in the world: he weaves body, metal, research, and institutional responsibility into a single identity.]
