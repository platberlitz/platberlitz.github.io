# Waveworn Phenomenon

## Definition

[A Waveworn Phenomenon is an abnormal state left by the Lament. The source entry frames it through aftereffects rather than through a single creature, faction, or technology. It can appear as fractured terrain, unstable frequencies, displaced ruins, altered matter, distorted time, strange memory effects, or locations where familiar physical behavior no longer holds. The useful rule is that a Waveworn Phenomenon is not just damage. It is damage that has become a condition, an environment, or a continuing distortion.]

## Physical and Spatial Signs

[The physical signs of a Waveworn Phenomenon may be obvious, such as broken land, displaced structures, or ruins that no longer fit their surroundings. They may also be subtler, such as matter behaving incorrectly or a place feeling unstable because its frequencies do not settle into a normal pattern. Source alignment matters here: the phenomenon is tied to the Lament's abnormal aftermath, not to a separate invented substance. Terrain can be fractured, ruins can be out of place, and the world can look as if the catastrophe bent the rules by which it was assembled.]

## Time, Memory, and Frequency

[The source also names distorted time and strange memory effects, which keeps Waveworn Phenomena from being only geological. A region can be waveworn because memory no longer rests cleanly inside people, objects, or locations. It can be waveworn because time feels damaged or repeated, or because frequencies that should have faded continue to interfere with the present. This makes the category especially important for lorebook use, since it can support scenes where investigation, survival, and perception are all affected by the same post-Lament abnormality.]

## Dossier Use

[A Waveworn Phenomenon should be written as evidence of the Lament still acting through the environment. It can create hazards, mysteries, research targets, or emotional unease, but it should remain grounded in the source list: fractured terrain, unstable frequency, displaced ruins, altered matter, distorted time, memory disturbance, and broken physical behavior. It connects naturally to Remnant Energy, Tacet Discord activity, Echo traces, and Resonator risk, but it should not be treated as a synonym for any one of those things.]
