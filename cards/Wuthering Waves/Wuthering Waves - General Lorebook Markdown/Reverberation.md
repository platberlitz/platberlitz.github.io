## Definition and Mechanism

[Reverberation is the feedback phenomenon that occurs when a Resonator overextends their capacity to channel and manipulate frequency waves. It is the resonant analogue of physical or mental exhaustion, manifesting as a cascading feedback loop within the Resonator's own vibrational field. When a Resonator pushes their abilities beyond their sustainable limits, whether in prolonged combat, repeated use of Resonance Liberation, or excessive Echo absorption, the frequencies they manipulate begin to resonate back upon themselves, creating dissonance rather than constructive harmony. This feedback effect can manifest in a variety of ways, ranging from mild discomfort and momentary disorientation to severe physical and psychological distress.]

## Physical Manifestations

[The physical symptoms of Reverberation vary depending on the severity and duration of the overexertion. In mild cases, a Resonator may experience trembling, blurred vision, ringing in the ears, or a sensation of internal vibration, as though their very cells are oscillating out of phase. More severe cases can lead to hemorrhaging, muscular spasms, temporary paralysis, and in extreme instances, loss of consciousness. The Resonator's Frequency Spectrum, normally a stable and coherent vibrational signature, becomes distorted and unstable, reducing their ability to perceive and manipulate frequencies effectively. This degradation in performance can be dangerous not only to the afflicted Resonator but also to their allies, as the destabilized frequencies can interfere with the resonance of nearby Resonators, creating a cascading failure effect.]

## Psychological Impact

[Reverberation is not limited to physical symptoms. The feedback effect can also disrupt a Resonator's mental state, leading to confusion, impaired judgment, emotional volatility, and in severe cases, temporary dissociation from reality. Some Resonators report experiencing vivid hallucinations or intrusive memories during episodes of severe Reverberation, suggesting that the feedback effect extends beyond the physical frequency field into the cognitive and emotional dimensions of consciousness. This psychological component makes Reverberation particularly insidious, as it can compromise a Resonator's ability to recognize their own deteriorating condition and take corrective action before the situation becomes critical.]

## Prevention and Mitigation

[Resonator training programs place significant emphasis on recognizing the early signs of Reverberation and implementing preventive measures. Resonators are taught to monitor their Resonance Energy reserves carefully, avoid unnecessary expenditure of high-cost abilities, and rotate out of combat before reaching their limits. The Concerto system, which allows Resonators to swap in and out of combat while triggering Intro and Outro skills, is one practical mechanism for distributing the resonant load across a team and preventing any single individual from bearing too much strain. Resonance Nexuses also provide essential recovery services, where injured or overtaxed Resonators can have their frequency fields stabilized and their Reverberation symptoms alleviated through targeted resonant therapy.]

## Chronic Reverberation

[Repeated or prolonged episodes of Reverberation, if not properly managed, can lead to chronic degradation of a Resonator's abilities. Over time, the cumulative damage from feedback effects can permanently alter a Resonator's Frequency Spectrum, reducing their range, sensitivity, and power. In the worst cases, chronic Reverberation can lead to total resonance failure, where the Resonator loses the ability to resonate with frequency waves entirely. This catastrophic outcome, while rare, underscores the importance of responsible resource management and the limits of what even the most powerful Resonator can safely endure.]
