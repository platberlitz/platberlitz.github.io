## Fundamental Bond

[The Resonance Chain is the invisible yet powerful bond that forms between a Resonator and another frequency-bearing entity, whether that entity is an Echo they have absorbed and equipped, a Tacet Discord they have encountered in combat, or, in rarer circumstances, another Resonator with whom they have established a deep resonant connection. This chain is not a physical tether but a vibrational link, a shared frequency channel through which information, energy, and intent can flow between the connected parties. It is the mechanism that makes the Echo system possible, the force that binds a Resonator to their power sources, and the foundation of all cooperative resonance between allies.]

## Echo Chains

[The most common and practically significant Resonance Chain is the one that forms between a Resonator and their equipped Echoes. When a Resonator absorbs the residual frequency left by a defeated Tacet Discord and integrates it into their own Frequency Spectrum, a Resonance Chain is established, connecting the Resonator's native frequency to the Echo's residual signature. This chain enables the Resonator to invoke the Echo's skills in combat, summon the Echo to aid them in battle, and benefit from the stat bonuses and Sonata effects that the Echo provides. The strength and stability of this chain depend on the compatibility between the Resonator's spectrum and the Echo's frequencies, with greater compatibility resulting in stronger, more reliable chains.]

## Tacet Discord Chains

[A Resonance Chain can also form under combat conditions between a Resonator and a living Tacet Discord, though this is typically a transient and antagonistic connection. During intense battles, the frequency fields of the combatants overlap and interfere with each other, creating momentary chains that can be exploited for tactical advantage. Some Resonators, particularly those with specialized abilities, can establish more deliberate chains with Tacet Discords, using them to disrupt the enemy's frequency field, extract information, or even temporarily control the creature's behavior. The Phantom Tacet Discord, a rare mutated variant found randomly in Solaris-3, is known to form particularly strong and unusual Resonance Chains, which is why defeating them unlocks special Phantom Echoes with unique appearances and abilities.]

## Inter-Resonator Chains

[In the context of team combat, Resonance Chains form naturally between allied Resonators through the Concerto system. As Resonators deal damage and perform dodges in combat, they accumulate Concerto Energy, and when they swap positions on the field, the exiting Resonator's Outro Skill and the incoming Resonator's Intro Skill are triggered in sequence. This handoff is mediated by a Resonance Chain that briefly links the two Resonators, allowing their frequencies to harmonize and combine for maximum effect. The strength of this inter-Resonator chain depends on the compatibility of the team members' Frequency Spectra and the timing of their swap, with well-coordinated teams achieving dramatically more powerful chain effects than poorly coordinated ones.]

## Chain Stability and Breakage

[Resonance Chains are not permanent and can be destabilized or broken by a variety of factors. Physical distance, resonance interference from hostile frequencies, excessive reverberation, or Corrosion can all weaken a chain, reducing the effectiveness of the bond and potentially severing it entirely. In the case of Echo chains, breaking the chain means the Resonator can no longer access that Echo's skills until the chain is re-established through re-absorption or resonant recalibration. In inter-Resonator chains, breakage can result in the loss of Concerto Energy buildup and the inability to execute coordinated Intro and Outro skills, severely compromising team effectiveness in high-stakes combat scenarios.]
