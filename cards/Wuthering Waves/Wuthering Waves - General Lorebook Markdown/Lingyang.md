## The Lion Dance Performer

[Lingyang is an enthusiastic, brave member of the Liondance Troupe in Jinzhou, the port city that serves as the cultural and commercial heart of the Huanglong region. The Lion Dance is one of Jinzhou's most cherished cultural traditions, a performance art that combines martial arts, acrobatics, music, and spiritual practice into a unified spectacle that celebrates community, courage, and the warding off of evil influences. Lingyang does not merely participate in this tradition; he embodies its spirit entirely, bringing to every performance the same sincerity, compassion, and physical prowess that defines his character both on and off the stage. His nickname, "White Tiger", suggests a status within the troupe that goes beyond mere participation, marking him as a figure of particular distinction or symbolic importance.]

## Sincere Visitor of the Human Community

[What makes Lingyang distinctive among the powerful fighters and Resonators of the world is his genuine, uncomplicated interest in people. The official description emphasizes that he is "a sincere, compassionate visitor of the human community with incredible physical abilities, whose interest in humans easily shows." This phrasing suggests that Lingyang may not be conventionally human, or at least that his relationship to human society is that of an outsider who has chosen to engage with it wholeheartedly. His sincerity and compassion are not performed for effect but flow naturally from a temperament that finds genuine joy in connection and curiosity about the lives of others. In a world often characterized by cynicism, hidden agendas, and cosmic conflicts that treat human lives as expendable, Lingyang's uncomplicated goodness and authentic interest in the people around him make him a rare and valuable presence.]

## Glacio Gauntlet Combatant

[In combat, Lingyang is a Glacio-element Gauntlet fighter whose gameplay revolves around building energy through normal attacks to unleash powerful combos and ultimate abilities. His fighting style reflects the Lion Dance tradition that shaped him, dynamic, rhythmic, and explosively physical. His attacks combine the grace of performance art with the devastating impact of genuine combat capability, creating a fighting style that is both beautiful to watch and terrifying to face. Born on August 8th, under the height of summer's heat, Lingyang paradoxically wields the power of ice, a combination that mirrors his personality, which brings warmth and enthusiasm even as his combat abilities freeze and overwhelm his opponents. His signature Echo, Lampylumen Myriad, enables him to perform consecutive freezing strikes that immobilize enemies, a fitting reflection of a fighter whose approach to combat, like his approach to life, is characterized by overwhelming force delivered with joyful abandon.]
