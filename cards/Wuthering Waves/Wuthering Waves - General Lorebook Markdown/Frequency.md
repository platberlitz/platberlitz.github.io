## Fundamental Nature

[Frequency refers to the energy waves that permeate the world of Solaris-3 and serve as the fundamental medium through which Resonators exercise their powers. These waves are not merely abstract constructs but tangible, measurable phenomena that saturate the environment, the creatures, and even the inanimate objects of the post-Lament world. Every action, every movement, and every interaction in Solaris-3 generates or is influenced by frequency waves, making them as essential to the physics of the world as gravity or electromagnetism. For Resonators, frequencies are both fuel and weapon, the raw material from which all their abilities are forged.]

## Classification and Types

[Frequencies in Solaris-3 are classified according to their elemental properties, which determine the nature of their effects when manipulated. The six primary frequency categories, Glacio, Fusion, Electro, Aero, Spectro, and Havoc, correspond to distinct bands of the vibrational spectrum, each with characteristic amplitudes, wavelengths, and behavioral patterns. Glacio frequencies are slow, oscillatory, and associated with cold and crystallization. Fusion frequencies are high-energy, explosive, and linked to heat and combustion. Electro frequencies are rapid, discontinuous, and manifest as lightning and electrical discharge. Aero frequencies are flowing, dynamic, and expressed through wind and air currents. Spectro frequencies are luminous, informational, and tied to light and knowledge. Havoc frequencies are chaotic, entropic, and associated with darkness, decay, and entropy.]

## Residual Frequencies and Echoes

[One of the most significant properties of frequencies is their persistence. When a creature such as a Tacet Discord is defeated, the frequencies that animated its existence do not simply vanish but linger in the environment as residual energy. Under the right conditions, these residual frequencies can crystallize into Echoes, fragments of the defeated creature's essence that can be absorbed by Resonators and incorporated into their own power set. This process is central to the Echo system in Wuthering Waves, as it allows Resonators to continuously expand their abilities by collecting and integrating new frequencies. The Data Bank system tracks the accumulation of these frequencies, with higher Data Bank levels increasing the probability of obtaining rare and powerful Echoes from defeated enemies.]

## Environmental Interaction

[Frequencies also interact with the physical environment in complex and sometimes unpredictable ways. Environmental hazards such as Toxic Spores, Vine Traps, and Corroders all emit disruptive frequencies that can interfere with a Resonator's perception and abilities. Conversely, resonant technology such as Resonance Beacons, Resonance Nexuses, and Sensors are designed to harmonize with environmental frequencies, enabling fast travel, area mapping, and the detection of hidden objects and enemies. Puzzles throughout Solaris-3, such as Capture Frequency Signal, Transmit Frequency Signal, and Sound Emulators, require Resonators to decode and manipulate specific frequency patterns, demonstrating that mastery of frequencies extends beyond combat into the realm of problem-solving and exploration.]

## Theoretical Implications

[The study of frequencies has profound implications for understanding the nature of reality in Solaris-3. The Lament, the catastrophic event that reshaped the world, is widely believed to have been a massive disruption in the global frequency field, unleashing chaotic waves that gave rise to the Tacet Discords and fundamentally altered the physics of the planet. Resonators, as individuals capable of perceiving and manipulating these waves, represent humanity's best hope for not only surviving in this altered reality but potentially restoring or at least stabilizing the disrupted frequency field. Research into frequencies continues across all Resonator organizations, with the ultimate goal of developing a comprehensive theory that can explain the Lament, predict future disruptions, and perhaps even reverse the damage that has been done.]
