# The Lament

## Source Position

[The Lament is not a single ordinary disaster with one neat boundary. In the source entry it is a family of catastrophic phenomena, meaning the term covers multiple expressions of catastrophe that can strike on different scales. A Lament can be global, regional, or otherwise localized, but every form carries the same basic implication: Solaris-3 can be changed at the level of land, memory, frequency, and physical law. The correct focus is catastrophe as an ongoing class of phenomena, not a named storm or one isolated historical accident.]

## Effects on Solaris-3

[The source describes the Lament as reshaping the world through altered physical law, damaged land, damaged memory, Remnant Energy, Waveworn Phenomena, and conditions that make Tacet Discords and Resonator crises common. This makes the Lament the upstream pressure behind many later categories of danger. It does not merely break cities or landscapes. It leaves abnormal states where matter, memory, and frequency can stop behaving as ordinary survivors expect. The world after the Lament is therefore unstable because reality itself has been wounded in more than one register.]

## Frequency Legacy

[Remnant Energy and Waveworn Phenomena are two of the clearest source-aligned ways to describe the Lament's aftermath. Remnant Energy preserves lingering traces of life, emotion, memory, and catastrophe after ordinary forms vanish. Waveworn Phenomena mark abnormal locations or states where familiar physical behavior no longer holds. Together they show that the Lament's legacy is not limited to ruins. It can remain as residue, distortion, strange terrain, displaced memory, altered matter, or unstable frequency conditions that continue to shape later encounters.]

## Living Consequences

[The Lament also creates the conditions in which Tacet Discords and Resonator crises become common. Tacet Discords are frequency-born beings that consume frequencies, imitate forms, and leave traces that may become Echoes. Resonators, whose abilities and identities are tied to frequency, are especially exposed to a world where frequency itself has been damaged. For dossier use, the Lament should be treated as the broad catastrophic source category behind post-Lament instability across Solaris-3, rather than as a file for unrelated old terms or invented disaster systems.]
