# Baizhi

[Baizhi is a researcher with a reserved manner, careful judgment, and strong reliance on observation, data, and study near Huaxu Academy's scientific world. The source entry frames her first through method rather than spectacle. She is someone who trusts what can be examined, measured, compared, and understood through disciplined inquiry. This makes her reserved quality more than a personality note. It supports the way she approaches uncertainty: slowly, carefully, and with a preference for conclusions earned through evidence.]
