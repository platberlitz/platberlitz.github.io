# Tacet Discords

## Definition

[Tacet Discords are beings formed from chaotic frequencies after the Lament. The source entry makes frequency, not ordinary biology, the center of their nature. They are not just animals, monsters, machines, or spirits, although their bodies may imitate those categories. They possess Tacet Cores and Reverberation Bodies, consume frequencies, and leave traces that can become Echoes. This plural source entry should be used for the overall phenomenon of Tacet Discords, separate from older singular or broad files that may contain unrelated classification assumptions.]

## Bodies and Cores

[A Tacet Discord has a Tacet Core and a Reverberation Body. The core marks it as a frequency-born entity with an internal organizing center, while the Reverberation Body is the manifested form sustained by resonant frequency rather than ordinary biology. That body can resemble living beings, objects, plants, animals, people, or stranger shapes depending on what frequencies are being imitated. The important point is that appearance does not make the entity natural. A beastlike Tacet Discord is still a Tacet Discord, and an objectlike one is still sustained by chaotic frequency.]

## Consumption and Imitation

[The source says Tacet Discords consume frequencies and imitate living beings or objects. This gives them both an ecological role and a disturbing expressive role in the world after the Lament. They feed on frequency, but they also repeat forms from the world they threaten. Whisperin and Murmurin entries sit on the humanlike side of this mimicry, Howlers imitate flora and fauna, Tranquilites imitate inorganic natural matter, and Staticoids imitate or parasitize man-made objects. Clamorlings remain less clearly understood by comparison.]

## Echo Traces

[When Tacet Discords decay or are defeated, their lingering frequency traces can become Echoes. This makes Tacet Discords dangerous not only as attackers but also as part of a wider Remnant Energy ecology. Their traces can persist, be studied, or be used by Resonators to imitate abilities and draw on stored patterns. A source-aligned dossier should therefore treat Tacet Discords as post-Lament frequency entities with bodies, cores, consumption habits, mimicry behavior, and aftertraces, not as a simple bestiary detached from the catastrophe that made them common.]
