# Staticoid

## Definition

[Staticoid-type Tacet Discords mimic or parasitize man-made objects. The source entry lists tools, structures, devices, and other artificial objects touched by chaotic frequency as the kinds of forms associated with them. This makes Staticoids distinct from Tacet Discord types that imitate human bodies, animals, plants, stone, or minerals. Their point of reference is artificial matter shaped by people, then distorted or occupied by post-Lament frequency.]

## Artificial Forms

[A Staticoid may resemble something built, used, carried, inhabited, or engineered. The source wording includes mimicry and parasitism, which allows two aligned readings. Some Staticoids may form a Reverberation Body that imitates an artificial object. Others may attach themselves to or infest man-made objects touched by chaotic frequency. In either case, the objectlike appearance should not be treated as safe. A familiar tool, structure, or device can become a surface through which Tacet Discord behavior enters daily life.]

## Contrast With Other Types

[Staticoids are easiest to understand by contrast. Whisperin and Murmurin point toward humanlike forms and behavior. Howlers point toward flora and fauna. Tranquilites point toward earth, stone, minerals, and nonliving natural matter. Staticoids point toward the made environment: tools, structures, devices, and other artificial objects. That boundary keeps source usage clear. A mineral imitation should not be called Staticoid only because it is hard, and a machine-like or tool-like imitation should not be filed under Tranquilite only because it seems nonliving.]

## Dossier Use

[For lorebook scenes, Staticoids make civilization itself feel vulnerable to chaotic frequency. They suggest that the post-Lament threat does not stop at wilderness, ruins, or monsters in open fields. It can reach objects people trust because those objects were built for use, shelter, work, or communication. A source-aligned dossier should keep the explanation grounded: the entity is a Tacet Discord type that mimics or parasitizes man-made objects, with a frequency-born nature and a connection to the same Echo and Remnant Energy ecology as other Tacet Discords.]
