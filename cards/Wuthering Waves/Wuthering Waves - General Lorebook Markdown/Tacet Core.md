## Definition

[A Tacet Core is the central, anchoring structure within a Tacet Discord. It is the nucleus of the creature's frequency, the point from which all of its resonant power radiates and to which its entire being is bound. The Tacet Core is not merely an organ in a biological sense; it is a crystallization of corrupted frequency, a dense knot of vibrational energy that gives the Tacet Discord its form, behavior, and destructive capability.]

## Role in Tacet Discord Biology

[Every Tacet Discord, from the smallest predator-class entities to the most massive Calamity-class beings like the Crownless or Tempest Mephis, possesses a Tacet Core. The Core maintains the coherence of the Tacet Discord's body, anchoring its frequency patterns against the natural entropy that would otherwise cause its form to dissolve. Without its Core, a Tacet Discord cannot sustain itself; destroying the Core is the only reliable method of permanently defeating such a creature. Attacks that damage the periphery of a Tacet Discord's body may weaken it, but the Core must be shattered or destabilized to achieve a true kill.]

## Echo Formation

[The destruction of a Tacet Core is also the mechanism that enables Echo formation. When the Core is broken, the Tacet Discord's remaining frequencies do not simply vanish; they collapse inward, condensing into a residual frequency imprint that can be absorbed by a Resonator. This Echo retains a fragment of the original Tacet Discord's power and can be summoned by the absorbing Resonator as a combat ability. The quality and strength of the resulting Echo depend on the Tacet Discord's power level and the compatibility between its Spectrum Pattern and the absorbing Resonator. This relationship makes the Tacet Core central not only to the cycle of combat but to the broader economy of Resonator advancement.]

## Research Significance

[The study of Tacet Cores is a major research priority for organizations like the Black Shores and the Huaxu Academy. Examining intact or partially intact Cores provides insight into the origins of Tacet Discords, the nature of Lament corruption, and the fundamental mechanics of resonance. Some researchers theorize that Tacet Cores and Resonator Fortes share a common vibrational origin, suggesting that the boundary between Resonator and Tacet Discord is less absolute than most people believe.]
