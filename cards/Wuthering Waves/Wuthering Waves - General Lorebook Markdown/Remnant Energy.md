# Remnant Energy

## Definition

[Remnant Energy is lingering frequency residue left by life, emotion, memory, and catastrophe. The source entry defines it through what remains after ordinary forms are gone. It is not simply fuel, debris, or magic in a generic sense. It is the persistence of frequency where something once lived, felt, suffered, broke, fought, or was transformed. Because Wuthering Waves treats frequency as a deep part of beings and places, Remnant Energy is a way for the past to remain active inside the present.]

## Where It Remains

[The source names unstable locations, disaster sites, Sonoro Spheres, Echo traces, Tacet Discord remains, and other places where frequency and memory endure after ordinary forms vanish. That list gives Remnant Energy a broad but bounded field. It may be found where catastrophe has pressed too hard on reality, where memories have gathered into unusual spaces, or where frequency-born entities have decayed and left patterns behind. It is especially important that Remnant Energy does not require a living body to continue. It can survive the loss of the form that first carried it.]

## Relation to Other Entries

[Remnant Energy connects the Lament, Waveworn Phenomena, Tacet Discords, and Echoes without replacing any of them. The Lament can produce or expose it. Waveworn locations may be saturated by it. Tacet Discord remains may carry it. Echoes can preserve usable patterns that belong to the same ecology of lingering frequency. For source-aligned writing, Remnant Energy is best treated as residue with memory-bearing and frequency-bearing qualities, not as a universal explanation for every supernatural event.]

## Dossier Use

[In a dossier, Remnant Energy should make a place or trace feel haunted by frequency rather than by ordinary history alone. It can explain why a battlefield, ruin, Sonoro Sphere, Tacet Discord remnant, or Echo trace still matters after visible events have ended. It also gives researchers, Resonators, and survivors a reason to approach old sites carefully. The residue may preserve information, danger, emotion, or instability, and its continued presence shows how the post-Lament world refuses to let all consequences fade cleanly into the past.]
