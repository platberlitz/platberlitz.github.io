## Nature and Origins

[Threnodians are terrifying beings born from Laments, the catastrophic frequency events that reshape reality and bring devastation to civilization. Unlike ordinary Tacet Discords, which are corrupted creatures emerging from Lament-affected environments, Threnodians are something far more profound: they are embodiments of the Lament's destructive will, given form and purpose. They represent the Lament's force made sentient, and their emergence signals a level of catastrophe that surpasses even the most severe Tacet Discord outbreaks.]

## Powers and Abilities

[Threnodians possess abilities that extend beyond physical destruction. They can destroy the human spirit itself, shattering willpower and reducing even the most determined Resonators to hollow shells. They feed on collapsed wills, drawing sustenance from the despair and broken resolve of those they encounter. This makes them not merely combat threats but existential horrors, entities whose presence corrodes the psychological and spiritual foundations of everyone nearby. Their power scales with the Lament that birthed them; the greater the catastrophic event, the more potent the Threnodian that emerges from it.]

## Role in Eschatology

[Within the belief system of the Fractsidus, Threnodians are not merely monsters but heralds of a new world order. The organization venerates these beings as agents of necessary destruction, believing that the next great Lament will bring Threnodians of unprecedented power who will cleanse the world and open a threshold to something beyond human civilization as it currently exists. The Reminiscence-class Echoes bearing the names "Threnodian - Leviathan" and "Threnodian - Voidborne Construct" suggest that past encounters with these entities have left deep resonant imprints, powerful enough to be harnessed by Resonators who can bear the weight of their frequencies.]

## Documented Encounters

[Recorded encounters with Threnodians are rare, as most who face them do not survive to report. The Black Shores' Tethys System tracks anomalous frequency signatures that may indicate Threnodian activity, but reliable intelligence is scarce. Each known Threnodian variant displays a unique Spectrum Pattern of extraordinary complexity, far exceeding the patterns of even Calamity-class Tacet Discords. Whether Threnodians can be permanently destroyed or merely dispersed back into the Lament frequency remains an open and terrifying question.]
