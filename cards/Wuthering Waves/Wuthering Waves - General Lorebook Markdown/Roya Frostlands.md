## Geography and Environment

[The Roya Frostlands comprise the frozen northern expanse of Solaris-3, stretching across tundra, glacial shelf, and permafrost highlands around the settlement of Lahai-Roi. The climate is defined by perpetual cold, with temperatures regularly dropping far below freezing across every season. Snowfields dominate the terrain, broken only by jagged icespike ridgelines, deep crevasses, and the occasional geothermal vent that offers brief respite from the killing cold. The environment itself is hostile to most forms of life, and only specialized Resonators adapted to Glacio frequencies can operate there for extended periods without succumbing to the Freezing condition, a dangerous environmental hazard that slows movement, saps stamina, and eventually immobilizes the unprepared. Underground cavern systems such as the Stalaglow Cavern provide shelter and contain unusual bioluminescent phenomena, suggesting deeper resonance activity beneath the frozen crust.]

## The Roya Tribe and History

[The Roya Frostlands take their name from the Roya Tribe, an indigenous people whose history is intertwined with the frozen north. The Roya developed cultural practices and resonance adaptations uniquely suited to survival in the extreme cold. Their traditions, recorded in fragmentary rune systems found across the Frostlands, speak of generations of endurance and a deep spiritual connection to the land's harsh beauty. Nivora dolls and other cultural artifacts recovered from the region suggest a rich domestic life persisted despite the unforgiving environment. The Roya Tribe's past remains partially obscure, but their legacy endures in place names, archaeological remnants, and the resilience of the communities that still inhabit the region around Lahai-Roi.]

## Void Storm Threats

[The Roya Frostlands face an ongoing and escalating threat from Void Storms, violent metaphysical weather events that tear at the boundary between reality and the Void. These storms manifest as swirling zones of residual Voidmatter that corrupt the environment, spawn dangerous Voidcreeps, and destabilize local resonance fields. The surface of the Frostlands is periodically scoured by these events, forcing settlements to develop protective infrastructure including Voidmatter forcefields and specialized suppression technology. The Soliskin phenomenon, pockets of warmth and stability generated by resonance, provides critical safe havens. Startorch Academy and the Spacetrek Collective have devoted substantial research to understanding and mitigating Void Storm activity in the region, recognizing that the Frostlands sit at a critical fault line between Solaris-3's normal space and the encroaching Void.]

## Strategic Importance

[Despite its hostility, the Roya Frostlands hold immense strategic value. The region contains rare resonance minerals, unique Tacet Discord specimens adapted to extreme cold, and data critical to understanding Void phenomena. Lahai-Roi serves as a forward operating base for multiple organizations, and the Frostlands function as a living laboratory for technologies from Exostrider development to glacier probe surveys. The frozen wastes are also home to dangerous Tacet Discord such as Frostbite Coleoids, Sabercat Prowlers, and the enigmatic Spacetrek Explorer constructs that roam the northern ice, making the region both a treasure trove and a killing ground for those unprepared for its challenges.]
