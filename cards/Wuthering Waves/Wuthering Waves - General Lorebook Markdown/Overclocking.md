## Definition and Mechanism

[Overclocking is a dangerous, uncontrolled state in which a Resonator loses stable command of their Forte or frequency output. The Resonator's internal frequency regulation breaks down, causing their power to surge beyond safe limits in violent, unpredictable eruptions. Overclocking is not a technique or ability that can be willingly invoked without extreme risk; it is a failure state, a loss of containment that endangers the Resonator and everyone nearby.]

## Causes and Triggers

[Overclocking can be triggered by severe emotional distress, physical trauma, exposure to Lament-adjacent frequency contamination, or simply by pushing a Forte beyond its sustainable limits. Resonators classified under the Mutation category of Rabelle's Curve are at heightened risk, as their frequency profiles already exhibit instability. In some documented cases, Resonators have Overclocked during moments of intense grief, rage, or desperation, their Forte responding to emotional overload with catastrophic power discharge. The Fractsidus has been known to deliberately provoke Overclocking in captured Resonators as part of its eschatological experiments.]

## Physical and Environmental Effects

[During Overclocking, the Resonator's Tacet Mark often glows, fractures, or spreads beyond its normal boundaries. Their Forte output spirals out of control, causing elemental eruptions, spatial distortions, or other phenomena depending on the Resonator's frequency type. The surrounding environment suffers collateral damage: terrain warps, structures crack, and nearby individuals risk injury from uncontrolled energy discharge or frequency contamination. In extreme cases, Overclocking has been known to create localized Lament-like conditions, with reality itself bending under the strain of unchecked resonance.]

## Recovery and Prevention

[Recovery from Overclocking requires external intervention, typically from other Resonators capable of frequency stabilization or from specialized suppressive technologies. The Black Shores maintains protocols and equipment for containing Overclocked Resonators, and organizations like the Midnight Rangers train their members in emergency stabilization techniques. Prevention centers on regular Rabelle's Curve monitoring, psychological support, and strict limitations on Forte exertion for at-risk individuals. Some Resonators, such as Danjin, exist in a perpetual state near the Overclocking threshold, requiring constant vigilance and self-regulation to avoid catastrophic loss of control.]
