## Definition

[The True Lament is the prophesied future disaster sought by the extremist organization Fractsidus. Unlike the Laments that have occurred throughout recorded history, which Fractsidus views as incomplete or preliminary, the True Lament is conceived as a final, total event that will fundamentally reshape the nature of reality itself. To Fractsidus followers, this is not an apocalypse to be feared but a threshold to be crossed, a necessary transformation that will end the current age and inaugurate something beyond human comprehension.]

## Fractsidus Doctrine

[At the heart of Fractsidus doctrine is the belief that Laments are not random catastrophes but phases in a cosmic process. Each successive Lament brings the world closer to the True Lament, and each one produces Threnodians of increasing power and significance. The organization's leadership interprets past Laments as rehearsals or preludes, with the Threnodians that emerged from them serving as scouts or heralds for the ultimate event. Phrolova, the Fractsidus Overseer, conducts her operations with this eschatological framework in mind, viewing every act of sabotage, manipulation, and violence as a step toward accelerating the True Lament's arrival.]

## The Threshold Concept

[The concept of the threshold is central to True Lament theology. Fractsidus teaches that the current world, with its Resonators, Tacet Discords, and struggling civilizations, is an intermediate state, a holding pattern that persists because the True Lament has not yet been fully realized. Crossing the threshold means accepting total dissolution of the existing order and embracing whatever emerges on the other side. This belief makes Fractsidus agents fanatically dedicated and nearly impossible to deter through conventional threats, as they regard death and destruction as temporary inconveniences in the face of cosmic inevitability.]

## Countermeasures

[The Black Shores, through the Tethys System and operatives like the Shorekeeper, monitors for signs that Fractsidus may be attempting to trigger or accelerate the True Lament. The organization's activities, including the deployment of agents like Scar and Schwarzloch, suggest active efforts to destabilize regions, provoke Lament-adjacent events, and weaken the institutional defenses that hold back catastrophic frequency events. Preventing the True Lament is considered the highest strategic priority for every major organization aware of Fractsidus's goals.]
