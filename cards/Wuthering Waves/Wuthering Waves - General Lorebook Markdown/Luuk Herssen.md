# Luuk Herssen

[Luuk Herssen is a Spectro-element Resonator who wields Gauntlets and serves as the head physician of the Resonator Nursing Unit at Startorch Academy in Lahai-Roi. Behind his warm bedside manner and counselor's gentle disposition lies a man shaped by a past steeped in blood and deception, a past he has neither forgotten nor allowed to dictate who he has become. His Forte, Phase Transition, grants him a unique relationship with space-time resonance that manifests as luminous golden veins beneath his skin. Affiliated simultaneously with Startorch Academy, the Spacetrek Collective, and Lollo Logistics, Luuk occupies an unusual cross-section of institutional power in the New Federation's sphere, trusted with the physical and psychological well-being of the next generation of Resonators.]

## Attending Doctor and Counselor

[Luuk Herssen's Resonator classification remains listed as "Unclear" in official Spacetrek Collective records, a deliberate ambiguity that may speak to the unconventional nature of his abilities, the classified circumstances of his intake, or simply the fact that his resonance profile defies easy categorization within standard frameworks. His title, Phase Transition, refers both to his Forte designation and to the thematic arc of his own life: a man who has undergone a fundamental shift in state, from one mode of existence to another. Operating under the jurisdiction of the New Federation and stationed within the nation of Lahai-Roi, Luuk holds the position of attending medical doctor and mental health counselor at Startorch Academy's Resonator Nursing Unit, making him simultaneously responsible for the physical treatment of injured or overtaxed Resonators and for their psychological care. This dual portfolio, body and mind, reflects an integrated approach to Resonator welfare that Startorch Academy, under President Lucilla's leadership, has come to depend upon. His role within the Academy extends far beyond bandaging wounds and filing prescriptions: he stands as one of the few figures on campus whom students and staff alike trust with their most vulnerable moments, from post-combat trauma recovery to the quiet anxieties that accompany life in an institution built atop the ruins of a Lament.]

## Appearance

[Luuk Herssen's appearance is immediately striking and carefully composed. His light-gold hair falls with pale green highlights that curl at the tips, creating a soft, almost sunlit effect that contrasts sharply with the intensity of his eyes. His irises are red, deep and vivid, gleaming like blood, carrying what observers describe as a "fleeting hint of danger" that contradicts his otherwise gentle demeanor. These blood-red eyes are perhaps the most visible remnant of whatever life he lived before medicine, a reminder that warmth and threat can coexist within the same gaze. As Startorch Academy's attending doctor, he wears a teal-and-white overcoat that places him squarely within the institution's professional visual language, and he is rarely seen without white gloves, an accessory that serves practical medical purposes but also reads as a deliberate barrier between his hands and the world, as though the surgeon's discipline has never fully left him. Most tellingly, golden veins run through his arms like liquid sunlight, branching beneath the skin in luminous patterns that glow brighter and radiate visible light when he activates his Resonance Ability. These veins are the external signature of Phase Transition, his power made visible, pulsing through his body in rhythms that match the elongated elliptical waveform documented in his Rabelle's Curve analysis.]

## Forte: Phase Transition

[Phase Transition is the Forte associated with Luuk Herssen, filed in the records of the Spacetrek Collective under Startorch Academy's designation. While the precise mechanics of his ability remain classified within the Collective's research archives, its name suggests a capacity for fundamental state-change, a power that operates at the boundary between one condition and another, whether that boundary is physical, temporal, or resonant in nature. The Rabelle's Curve Fluctuation Stability Test performed on Luuk revealed a resonant signature characterized by an elongated elliptical waveform with considerable amplitude, indicating raw power of significant magnitude. Crucially, however, the test also documented a clear periodic rhythm within this high-amplitude pattern, and confirmed that Resonant Criticality thresholds have not been approached, meaning that despite the substantial energy his Forte channels, Luuk maintains stable control over his resonance and does not risk the catastrophic loss-of-self associated with criticality events. This combination of high power and stable periodicity is unusual. Most Resonators whose waveforms exhibit such amplitude tend to show signs of instability approaching criticality, but Luuk's curve remains clean and rhythmically predictable, suggesting a depth of self-regulation that may stem from his medical training, his psychological discipline, or the peculiar nature of Phase Transition itself. When activated, the golden veins running through his arms illuminate with intensifying radiance, suggesting that his Forte channels space-time resonance through his own circulatory or nervous system in a manner that renders his body itself a conduit and resonator.]

## Personality

[To the students and staff of Startorch Academy, Luuk Herssen presents as warm, approachable, and fundamentally kind, a doctor who listens more than he speaks, who treats every patient with genuine attention, and who maintains an unflappable composure even in the aftermath of combat injuries, Resonance overload incidents, or the emotional crises that afflict young Resonators far from home. This warmth is not a performance, or at least not entirely: those who know him well describe a man whose friendliness is authentic and whose care for others is real. Yet beneath this gentle exterior lies a past defined by blood and lies, specifics that Luuk has never fully disclosed but that occasionally surface in fragments of conversation, in the way his red eyes narrow at certain topics, or in the tension that Aemeath once observed in his bearing before Rover's visit to Lahai-Roi changed something in him. What defines Luuk most is not this dark history but his active, relentless refusal to be determined by it. He has committed himself to a philosophy of cutting open lies to reveal the truth within, a conviction that manifests both in his medical practice (diagnosis as an act of revelation, peeling away symptoms to find root causes) and in his approach to the political and personal deceptions that surround him. He is a man who understands darkness intimately and has chosen, deliberately and daily, to practice light.]

## History

[Luuk Herssen's history before his appointment at Startorch Academy is shrouded in deliberate obscurity, described in official records only as "a mysterious past" and in personal fragments as "blood and lies." The most revealing surviving fragment, catalogued under the heading "Brother", describes a scene from what appears to be his childhood: a cold light glaring deep into the night, Luuk sitting beneath it and holding out his hand without protest while a man he identifies as his "father" grips his palm, the man's hand still unmistakably that of a child. This haunting image suggests that Luuk's upbringing involved coercive or experimental conditions, possibly related to Resonance training, military conditioning within the New Federation's institutional apparatus, or involvement with organizations whose methods blurred the line between cultivation and exploitation. His adoptive brother, Rhein Herssen, shared this environment, and the bond between them was forged in whatever crucible their childhood represented. Luuk's transition from whatever he was before to the doctor he became involved a deliberate reinvention, a Phase Transition in the most literal sense. Whether he served as a combatant, an operative, or a test subject in his earlier life, he eventually chose medicine as his vocation, bringing to it the same intensity and precision that presumably characterized his previous work. His integration into Startorch Academy, the Spacetrek Collective, and Lollo Logistics suggests that his rehabilitation was not solitary but institutionally supported, possibly facilitated by figures who recognized his value and his capacity for change. The arrival of Rover in Lahai-Roi appears to have marked another turning point: Aemeath's observations indicate that Luuk carried a constant tension before this event, as if bracing for battle at any moment, and that the change in him, a relaxation, a settling into his medical identity more fully, coincided with Rover's presence and the events their visit set in motion.]

## Affiliations

[Luuk Herssen's web of affiliations places him at a distinctive intersection of Lahai-Roi's institutional landscape. Startorch Academy is his primary professional home, where he leads the Resonator Nursing Unit and serves as both physician and counselor. The Spacetrek Collective, Lahai-Roi's premier research organization dedicated to space-time discovery and the understanding of the fractured world beyond ordinary boundaries, claims Luuk as well, likely in a capacity that bridges their medical research division with his clinical practice. Lollo Logistics, the third organization in his portfolio, represents a more commercial or operational affiliation, suggesting that Luuk's skills and knowledge extend beyond the Academy's walls into the practical infrastructure that supports Lahai-Roi's operations. His origin in the New Federation and his current station within the nation of Lahai-Roi place him in a cross-cultural position, shaped by the Federation's military and institutional traditions but now operating within Lahai-Roi's more research-oriented and survival-focused society. His connection to the broader conflict against Fractsidus, and specifically to the figure of Schwarzloch (the Grand Architect), adds a layer of unresolved enmity to his profile: Luuk has spoken of wrenching the entirety of Fractsidus from their roots and casting them into the abyss they carved, suggesting that his "past of blood" may be directly entangled with the extremist organization's machinations. This combination of healer, researcher, and someone with a personal vendetta against one of the world's most dangerous factions makes Luuk Herssen one of the most complex figures operating within the Startorch Academy ecosystem, a man whose present compassion is inseparable from the violence he has survived and the justice he still intends to see fulfilled.]
