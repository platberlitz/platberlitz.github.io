## Overview

[The Tethys System is the Black Shores' primary predictive and analytical platform for monitoring, assessing, and responding to Lament threats across Solaris-3. It functions as a vast network of data collection, pattern analysis, and decision-support tools, integrating information from Resonance Spectrum Pattern readings, Tacet Discord activity reports, environmental frequency measurements, and historical Lament data to produce actionable intelligence for the Black Shores' operations.]

## Capabilities

[The Tethys System watches patterns across multiple data streams simultaneously. It tracks resonance activity in real time, detecting anomalies that may indicate emerging Tacet Discord outbreaks, frequency contamination events, or the early signatures of Lament formation. The system supports decision-making at every level of the Black Shores' command structure, from field operatives who receive localized threat assessments to the Shorekeeper herself, who uses Tethys analysis to guide strategic priorities. The system can correlate Spectrum Pattern data from thousands of sources to identify trends invisible to individual analysts.]

## Operational Integration

[Tethys Terminals exist as physical installations within the Black Shores' infrastructure and in allied territories. These terminals serve as access points for the system, allowing authorized personnel to query threat data, initiate cleansing protocols against frequency corruption, and coordinate responses across multiple regions. The terminals also function as portal systems in some contexts, opening pathways to Lament-affected zones or other critical areas. The system's integration with Resonance Beacon networks allows it to maintain continuous coverage across vast geographic areas, using the beacons as both sensor platforms and communication relays.]

## Strategic Importance

[The Tethys System is considered one of the Black Shores' most vital assets. Its predictive capabilities have prevented or mitigated numerous Lament-adjacent events, and its analytical output informs everything from Resonator deployment schedules to research priorities. The system's existence is itself classified information, known only to senior Black Shores personnel and select allies. Fractsidus has made the Tethys System a target of sabotage and infiltration efforts, recognizing that blinding the Black Shores' primary intelligence platform would significantly advance their goal of triggering the True Lament.]
