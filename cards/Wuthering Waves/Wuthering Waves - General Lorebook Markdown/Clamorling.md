# Clamorling

## Definition

[Clamorling-type Tacet Discords are a poorly understood Tacet Discord class. The source entry is explicit about the limits of knowledge: their classification remains incomplete compared with clearer mimicry types such as Whisperin, Howler, Tranquilite, and Staticoid. That uncertainty is the most important source-aligned feature. A Clamorling dossier should not pretend the class has a fully mapped origin, behavior chart, or visible rule set when the source says the opposite.]

## Classification Gap

[The comparison to clearer types gives the shape of the problem. Whisperin and Murmurin relate to humanlike traces, Howlers imitate flora or fauna, Tranquilites imitate inorganic natural matter, and Staticoids mimic or parasitize man-made objects. Clamorling does not receive that kind of stable mimicry description in the source. It is still a Tacet Discord class, but the evidence available to the lorebook leaves its exact criteria unresolved. The class represents the limits of current categorization in a world where chaotic frequency keeps producing exceptions.]

## Handling Uncertainty

[Because Clamorlings are poorly understood, the safest writing approach is disciplined restraint. They can be mentioned as a recognized but incomplete class, and they can appear as part of Tacet Discord taxonomy, but specific claims about appearance, ecology, intelligence, or behavior should be avoided unless supported by another source entry. Uncertainty can still be useful. It makes Clamorling a marker for field reports that do not fit known types, research files with open questions, and encounters where the classification system itself fails to settle the danger.]

## Dossier Use

[A source-aligned Clamorling dossier should communicate that knowledge has a boundary. The post-Lament world contains Tacet Discords whose mimicry can be named, but it also contains classes where the name precedes full understanding. Clamorling belongs in that second category. It should remain connected to Tacet Discord fundamentals, such as chaotic frequency formation, Reverberation Bodies, frequency consumption, and possible Echo traces, while preserving the incomplete status that distinguishes it from the better described mimicry classes around it.]
