## Definition and Nature

[A Resonator is a human being who possesses the extraordinary capacity to resonate with Frequency Waves permeating the environment of Solaris-3. This resonance is not merely a passive sensitivity but an active channel through which the Resonator can perceive, manipulate, and weaponize the invisible vibrational energies that course through the world. Unlike ordinary humans, whose bodies and senses are deaf to these frequencies, a Resonator's physiology is fundamentally attuned to the harmonic undercurrents of reality itself, making them uniquely suited to interact with the forces that shape the post-Lament world.]

## Role in Society

[In the fractured civilization of Solaris-3, Resonators occupy a critical societal role. They serve as both protectors and explorers, venturing into territories overrun by Tacet Discords and other hostile entities that emerged after the Lament. Their ability to resonate allows them to combat these threats directly, absorbing the residual frequencies left behind by defeated enemies and converting them into Echoes, which further amplify their power. Resonators are also essential to the operation of key infrastructure such as Resonance Beacons and Resonance Nexuses, devices that form the backbone of fast travel, area mapping, and recovery systems across the world.]

## Combat Abilities

[Resonators wield a diverse arsenal of abilities powered by their resonance. Their elemental affinities, ranging across Glacio, Fusion, Electro, Aero, Spectro, and Havoc, determine the nature of their offensive and defensive capabilities. In combat, Resonators build Resonance Energy by dealing damage or executing precise dodges, and once this energy is full, they unleash their Resonance Liberation, a devastating ultimate technique unique to each individual. Additionally, Resonators can chain their powers through the Concerto system, accumulating Concerto Energy in battle and triggering Intro Skills and Outro Skills when swapping between allies on the field, creating synergistic combinations.]

## Echoes and Power Sources

[One of the most distinctive aspects of a Resonator's power is their ability to absorb and equip Echoes. When a Tacet Discord is defeated, the remaining frequency may crystallize into an Echo, a fragment of the vanquished creature's essence. A Resonator can equip these Echoes and invoke their Echo Skills in combat, effectively borrowing the abilities of their former foes. This mechanic transforms every encounter into both a fight for survival and an opportunity for growth, as the Resonator continuously expands their repertoire by integrating new frequencies into their arsenal.]

## Variability Among Resonators

[No two Resonators resonate in exactly the same way. Individual differences in innate aptitude, elemental alignment, weapon proficiency, and personal history give rise to an enormous diversity in how resonance manifests. Some Resonators excel as frontline damage dealers, while others specialize in support, healing, or tactical control. The Rover, for example, is described as a mysterious Resonator with the power to absorb all sounds, underscoring how resonance abilities can take profoundly unusual forms. This variability is both a strength and a source of complexity for Resonator organizations tasked with coordinating their efforts against the threats facing Solaris-3.]
