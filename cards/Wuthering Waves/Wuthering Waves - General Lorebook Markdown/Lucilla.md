## President of Startorch Academy

[Lucilla holds the position of President at Startorch Academy, the prestigious educational institution located in Lahai-Roi. The Academy itself serves as one of the world's primary centers for the study of Resonance, bringing together students from diverse backgrounds and regions to develop their understanding of elemental power, combat technique, and the broader philosophical and practical applications of Resonance theory. As President, Lucilla bears responsibility not merely for the administrative functioning of the institution but for its educational philosophy, its ethical standards, and its role in shaping the next generation of Resonators who will face the challenges of an increasingly dangerous and unpredictable world.]

## The Idealist with Both Feet on the Ground

[The official characterization of Lucilla as "an idealist with both feet on the ground who holds quiet vigil over her students as they rise to challenge the very sky" captures a tension that defines her leadership style and personal philosophy. She is an idealist, someone who believes in the possibility of a better world, in the power of education to transform lives, and in the potential of young people to exceed the limitations that their elders have accepted as permanent. Yet she is not a naive or detached idealist; her feet remain firmly planted on the ground, her vision tempered by practical understanding of the obstacles her students will face and the costs that idealism inevitably exacts. The quiet vigil she maintains over her students is not the surveillance of a controlling authority but the watchful care of someone who knows what it costs to grow into one's power and who is determined to provide whatever protection and guidance she can offer.]

## Glacio Rectifier and Protector

[As a Glacio-element Rectifier wielder, Lucilla's combat role reflects her institutional role, that of a supporter and protector who enables others to achieve their full potential while providing the defensive framework within which growth can safely occur. Rectifiers in Wuthering Waves are associated with support, amplification, and the channeling of energy for purposes beyond mere destruction. Lucilla's specific capabilities as a 5-star character suggest that her power is considerable, but that she has chosen to apply it in service of institutional leadership rather than personal glory or direct combat engagement. Her decision to remain at the Academy, guiding and protecting her students rather than pursuing a more publicly heroic path, speaks to a character who understands that the most important battles are often fought not on the battlefield but in the classroom, the mentorship relationship, and the quiet moments of encouragement that determine whether a young person will fulfill their potential or fall short of it.]
