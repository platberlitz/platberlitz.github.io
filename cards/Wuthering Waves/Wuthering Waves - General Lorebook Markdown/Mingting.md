# Mingting

## Definition

[Mingting is the capital of Huanglong and the seat of important ministries and political institutions. The source entry identifies it through governance, administration, and the convergence of high-level authority. It should therefore be treated as more than a city name. Mingting represents the institutional center through which Huanglong's ministries, policy concerns, security judgments, and public order questions can meet. When a dossier references Mingting, it is invoking the capital's administrative weight as much as its physical location.]

The city is where public questions can become administrative action, and where the consequences of regional crises may be weighed by people responsible for law, security, justice, and continuity.]
