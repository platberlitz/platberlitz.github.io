## Governance and Spiritual Authority

[The Tetragon Temple serves as both the governing institution and the spiritual heart of Septimont, exercising authority that blends the political and the prophetic in a single institutional framework. Its governance extends across civil administration, judicial functions, and the maintenance of Septimont's unique cultural and spiritual traditions. Unlike purely secular governments or purely religious institutions, the Temple operates in the space where political authority derives from spiritual legitimacy and where religious doctrine shapes practical policy. This fusion of roles creates a governance model where decisions are made not merely on the basis of practical benefit but in accordance with prophetic insight and spiritual imperatives that may not always align with conventional political logic.]

## Prophetic Traditions

[The Tetragon Temple maintains its own prophetic traditions, distinct from but potentially related to those of the Order of the Deep in Rinascita. These traditions involve the interpretation of signs, the reading of resonance patterns, and the maintenance of sacred practices that connect Septimont's present to its foretold future. The prophetic function of the Temple gives its leadership access to forms of knowledge unavailable to ordinary political institutions, though whether this knowledge is genuinely supernatural or simply the product of sophisticated resonance analysis remains a question the Temple has no incentive to answer definitively.]

## Iuno, the Defiant Priestess

[Iuno serves as a priestess of the Tetragon Temple, embodying its values while simultaneously challenging the destinies that prophecy might seek to impose. Her introduction describes her as favored by fate under one name and swept away by it in another, yet never once defeated nor ever yielded. She is the defiant Priestess, one who walks the intricate web of destiny and becomes the only answer, suggesting that her relationship with the Temple's prophetic traditions is one of active negotiation rather than passive acceptance. As a five-star Aero Gauntlet wielder who alternates between melee moon ring and ranged moon bow combat states, her fighting style reflects the Temple's own duality, grounded in established practice yet reaching toward something beyond it. Her presence within the Temple hierarchy demonstrates that prophetic institutions can contain and even be strengthened by those who question rather than merely obey.]
