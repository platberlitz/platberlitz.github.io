# Jinzhou

## Overview

[Jinzhou is a frontier city within the nation of Huanglong, serving as one of the most important settlements in the post-Lament world of Solaris-3. Positioned at the edge of civilized territory, Jinzhou represents both the resilience of human civilization and the constant struggle against the encroaching dangers of the wilderness. The city is governed by its Magistrate and protected by the Sentinel Jué, whose guardian presence provides a measure of security that allows the city's inhabitants to live, work, and build communities despite the ever-present threat of Tacet Discords.]

## Leadership and Governance

[Jinzhou is led by Jinhsi, who serves simultaneously as the city's Magistrate and as the Sentinel's Appointed Resonator. Jinhsi is described as one who gently brightens the hopes of her people, like rays of winter sunlight, displaying humility and wholehearted commitment to guiding her people toward a brilliant future. This dual role as magistrate and Sentinel's Appointed gives Jinhsi authority over both the temporal and spiritual affairs of the city, making her one of the most powerful and respected figures in the region. Her leadership style emphasizes compassion, accessibility, and a genuine connection to the people she serves, qualities that have earned her the deep trust and admiration of Jinzhou's residents.]

## Strategic Importance

[As a frontier city, Jinzhou occupies a position of immense strategic importance within Huanglong and the wider world of Solaris-3. The city serves as a bulwark against the Tacet Discord threats that lurk in the wilderness, a base of operations for Resonators who venture beyond the city walls to combat these creatures, and a hub of trade and communication between the settled heartlands of Huanglong and the frontier territories. Jinzhou's position at the boundary between safety and danger makes it a natural gathering point for Resonators, adventurers, and those seeking to make their mark in a world still recovering from catastrophe. The city's fortifications and defensive infrastructure reflect its role as a shield for the nation.]

## Notable Figures

[Jinzhou is home to or closely associated with many significant characters in the world of Wuthering Waves. Beyond Magistrate Jinhsi herself, the city is connected to Sanhua, a Glacio sword wielder with ice construct abilities; Baizhi, a healer accompanied by a pale creature; Lingyang, a powerful Glacio gauntlet fighter; and numerous other Resonators who operate in the frontier territories. The city also serves as a waypoint for the Rover, whose mysterious journey to recover lost memories inevitably leads them through Jinzhou and into contact with its leaders and protectors. The presence of Sentinel Jué further elevates the city's importance.]

## Daily Life and Culture

[Life in Jinzhou reflects the dual nature of frontier existence, the normal rhythms of trade, craftsmanship, and community coexist with the constant awareness of danger and the need for vigilance. The city's markets are active with commerce in Shell Credits, ascension materials, and other goods essential to Resonators and ordinary citizens alike. Cultural practices centered around the reverence of Sentinel Jué are woven into daily life, with ceremonies and traditions that honor the Sentinel's protection. The people of Jinzhou are characterized by their hardiness, their communal bonds, and their determination to build a future in a world that remains dangerous and unpredictable.]
