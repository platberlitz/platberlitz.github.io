## The Threnodian of the Deep

[Leviathan is the Threnodian entity associated with Rinascita and the historical phenomenon of the Dark Tide. As a Threnodian, it occupies a category of existence that transcends ordinary Tacet Discord, representing not merely a corrupted frequency but something closer to a sentient embodiment of specific resonance patterns and cosmic principles. Leviathan's connection to the Dark Tide suggests it is either the source or the primary expression of the sea-born corruption that periodically devastates Rinascita, making it one of the most significant existential threats documented on Solaris-3.]

## Dual Legacy in Cartethyia

[Leviathan's most significant impact on the current era manifests through Cartethyia, who carries both Sentinel and Threnodian legacy inside her. This dual inheritance means that Cartethyia contains within her not only the protective, guardian aspects of the Sentinel tradition but also the destructive, transformative power associated with Leviathan and the Threnodian lineage. The coexistence of these contradictory forces within a single being creates an internal tension that defines Cartethyia's existence and shapes her choices. Her former role as Blessed Maiden positioned her as the vessel through which these forces could be channeled and controlled, but her departure from that role suggests the burden became unsustainable or the purpose behind it became unacceptable.]

## Echo and Remembrance

[Leviathan's legacy persists even in physical form through the Reminiscence: Threnodian - Leviathan echo, a combat manifestation that allows Resonators to channel a fragment of its power. This echo grants the Flamewing's Shadow sonata effect, suggesting that Leviathan's influence extends even into the combat capabilities of those who engage with its remnants. The fact that such an echo exists and can be used suggests that Leviathan, while perhaps currently dormant or absent, has left traces throughout the resonance ecology that can be accessed and wielded by those with sufficient capability. This persistence of influence even in fragmentary form demonstrates the depth of Leviathan's impact on Solaris-3's metaphysical landscape.]
