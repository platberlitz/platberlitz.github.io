## Definition

[A Resonance Spectrum Pattern describes the unique frequency signature emitted by a Resonator, Tacet Discord, or any other resonant entity within the world of Solaris-3. Just as a fingerprint identifies a person in pre-Lament forensic science, a Spectrum Pattern identifies a resonance source with high specificity. These patterns are measurable, recordable, and comparable, forming the foundation of identification, compatibility analysis, and research across all organizations that study resonant phenomena.]

## Applications in Identification

[Spectrum Patterns are routinely used to identify unknown Resonators, classify Tacet Discords by type and threat level, and verify the provenance of Echo phenomena. When a Resonator registers with a governing body, their Spectrum Pattern is recorded alongside their Tacet Mark data and Rabelle's Curve classification, creating a composite profile. In the field, portable scanning devices can read ambient Spectrum Patterns to determine whether a Resonator or Tacet Discord was recently present in an area, a technique employed extensively by the Black Shores and the Midnight Rangers during investigations.]

## Compatibility and Resonance Pairing

[Beyond identification, Spectrum Patterns govern compatibility between Resonators and Echoes, between Resonators and certain resonance-based technologies, and potentially between Resonators themselves. A Resonator's ability to absorb and utilize an Echo depends partly on the alignment between their personal Spectrum Pattern and the Tacet Discord's residual frequencies. High compatibility yields stronger Echo Skills and more stable integration; low compatibility can render an Echo unusable or produce erratic effects. The Tethys System operated by the Black Shores analyzes Spectrum Pattern data at scale to detect emerging threats and predict Lament activity.]

## Tacet Discord Patterns

[Tacet Discords emit Spectrum Patterns that are often distorted, chaotic, or aggressively dominant versions of natural resonance. The patterns of higher-order Tacet Discords like the Crownless, Tempest Mephis, or Impermanence Heron are complex enough to fill entire research volumes. The study of these patterns has revealed that Tacet Discords are not random phenomena but structured expressions of corrupted frequency, tied ultimately to the Lament events that spawned them.]
