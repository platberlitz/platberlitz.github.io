## Overview

[The Sentinels are immensely powerful guardian entities that serve as the protectors of Solaris-3, a planet ravaged by the catastrophic event known as the Lament. These beings exist on a plane of power far beyond ordinary Resonators, wielding abilities that can shape the very fabric of reality and defend entire civilizations from existential threats. Their presence is woven deeply into the mythology, governance, and spiritual life of the nations that inhabit the world.]

## Role in the World

[Each Sentinel is traditionally associated with a particular region or nation, acting as a divine patron and guardian. The bond between a Sentinel and its people is not merely symbolic; it is a tangible connection that provides protection and guidance. Sentinels are revered as near-divine figures, and their appointed Resonators, those chosen to serve as intermediaries between the Sentinel and the mortal world, hold positions of great political and spiritual authority. In Jinzhou, for example, Jinhsi serves as the Sentinel's Appointed Resonator, a title that confers both the responsibility of leadership and the sacred duty of interpreting the Sentinel's will for the people.]

## Power and Nature

[The Sentinels possess powers that transcend the six known elements of Resonance, Glacio, Fusion, Electro, Aero, Spectro, and Havoc. Their abilities are tied to the fundamental frequencies of Solaris-3 itself, and they are capable of feats that defy conventional understanding of Resonance. The Spectro element, associated with light and knowledge, appears closely linked to several Sentinels, as evidenced by Jinhsi's Spectro affinity and her role as the Sentinel's Appointed. Jué, one of the Sentinels associated with the Jinzhou region, manifests as a serpentine dragon-like being capable of summoning thunderbolts and dealing devastating Spectro damage, reflecting the extraordinary scale of Sentinel power.]

## Sentinels and the Lament

[The Lament, the apocalyptic disaster that transformed Solaris-3 and unleashed the Tacet Discords upon the world, is central to the Sentinels' purpose. These guardians arose or were awakened in response to the cataclysm, taking on the role of shielding what remained of civilization from the encroaching darkness. The Tacet Discords, monstrous creatures born from the Lament, represent an ever-present threat that the Sentinels must constantly guard against. Their vigilance has allowed nations like Huanglong to survive and even thrive in the post-Lament world, though the cost of this protection is a burden that weighs heavily upon both the Sentinels and their chosen Resonators.]

## Known Sentinels

[Among the known Sentinels, Jué stands as the guardian associated with the Jinzhou region, a frontier city within the nation of Huanglong. Jué's power is immense, manifesting as a celestial dragon that commands lightning and radiant energy. Other Sentinels are believed to exist across the various nations and territories of Solaris-3, though their identities and domains are not all publicly known. The relationship between different Sentinels and the degree to which they cooperate or communicate with one another remains one of the great mysteries of the world.]
