## The Star Solaris

[Solaris is the star at the center of the stellar system that bears its name, and it is the celestial body around which the planet Solaris-3 orbits as its third planetary body. The designation "SOL3" used throughout the world's military and scientific frameworks derives directly from this stellar nomenclature, with the planet's full formal designation indicating its position as the third world from the star Solaris. The star serves as the primary source of light and energy for the entire system, and its presence is woven into the cultural, scientific, and spiritual fabric of the civilizations that inhabit Solaris-3. The broader stellar system is referred to as the Solisylum, a term that encompasses all celestial bodies bound by Solaris's gravitational influence.]

## Cultural and Scientific Significance

[The star Solaris holds profound significance for the inhabitants of Solaris-3, both as a practical astronomical reference and as a symbol embedded deeply in the world's cultural consciousness. The naming conventions of the planet itself, Solaris-3, directly honor the star, suggesting that the civilizations of this world organized their astronomical understanding around Solaris as the central and defining feature of their sky. Items and phenomena bearing the prefix "Soli" appear throughout the world, including Soliskin, a type of resource or energy associated with the star's radiance that can be found in various regions such as the Solvein Canopy. Soliskin Puffs, Soliskin Platforms, and Solight Dials represent technology and natural phenomena tied to solar energy, indicating that the light of the star Solaris is not merely passive illumination but a tangible force that can be harvested, channeled, and utilized by the people of Solaris-3.]

[The adventure known as "Stars of the Solisylum" further reinforces the importance of the broader stellar system in the cosmological understanding of the world's inhabitants. This suggests that the people of Solaris-3 possess awareness of other celestial bodies within their system and have developed frameworks for mapping and studying them. Whether through ancient tradition or modern scientific inquiry, the star Solaris and its system serve as a fixed point of orientation for a civilization that has otherwise been profoundly destabilized by the Lament.]

## Role in the Post-Lament World

[In the era following the Lament, the star Solaris takes on additional layers of meaning. The catastrophic event that reshaped the planet introduced forces fundamentally alien to the natural order of the system, including the Voidmatter that now contaminates vast swaths of the planet's surface and the Tacet Discords that emerged in the Lament's wake. The contrast between the natural, life-giving energy of the star Solaris and the corrupting, reality-distorting influence of the Void has become a central tension in the world's ongoing struggle. Soliskin and related solar-derived resources may serve as counterbalances to Voidmatter contamination, representing the star's enduring protective influence over its planetary system. The ongoing presence of the star in the sky, constant and unwavering, serves as a reminder to the people of Solaris-3 that their world existed in harmony long before the Lament, and that perhaps it may one day find such harmony again. The star Solaris thus stands as both a scientific reality and a symbol of endurance against the encroaching darkness that the Lament unleashed upon the third planet in its orbit.]
