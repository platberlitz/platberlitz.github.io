## A Vanished Land

[Ashinohara is a land that no longer exists in any conventional sense, having vanished from the world of Solaris-3 through circumstances that remain shrouded in mystery and sorrow. It survives now primarily through memory, tradition, and the living legacy of its last daughter, Hiyuki, the Miko of Flaming Sakura. The land was characterized by its miko traditions, a spiritual culture centered on shrine maidens who served as intermediaries between the human world and forces beyond ordinary perception. Ashinohara's connection to sacred fire, wishes entrusted across generations, and a flame said to have been tended by divine hands suggests a deeply spiritual civilization whose practices were rooted in maintaining balance between worlds.]

## Sacred Flame and Divine Tradition

[At the heart of Ashinohara's spiritual tradition was a sacred flame, a fire described as having been tended by divine hands in ages past. This flame was not merely symbolic but represented a living connection between the mortal realm and whatever divine forces Ashinohara's people recognized. The miko served as keepers of this flame, maintaining rituals that ensured its continuation and channeling its power into protective and spiritual functions. The tradition of entrusting wishes across generations, a practice where the hopes and prayers of ancestors were carried forward through successive miko, created a spiritual continuity that bound past, present, and future into a single unbroken thread of devotion and duty.]

## Hiyuki's Legacy

[Hiyuki, now residing in Lahai-Roi as the last and only member of the Special Response Force, carries the full weight of Ashinohara's legacy within her. As the Miko of Flaming Sakura, she is both priestess and living archive of a vanished civilization. Her Glacio resonance, manifested through sword combat, represents a transformation of Ashinohara's fire traditions into something adapted for the frozen north where she now serves. The Redbell flowers required for her ascension materials and the item called Our Choice suggest deep personal significance tied to her homeland. Every technique she wields, every resonance frequency she channels, carries within it the memory of Ashinohara's sacred flame and the wishes of generations of miko who came before her. She is not merely a survivor but the continuation of a tradition that would otherwise be extinguished forever.]
