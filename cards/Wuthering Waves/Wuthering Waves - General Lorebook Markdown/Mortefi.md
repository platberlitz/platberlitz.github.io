# Mortefi

## Identity

[Mortefi is a Mutant-class Resonator aligned with the Fusion frequency, a pistol user, and the bearer of the title Dragon's Breath. He originates from the New Federation, but his active institutional identity is rooted in Jinzhou, where he works at Huaxu Academy. Within the Academy he serves as the head of the Branch of Tacetite Weaponry under the Department of Safety, a position that immediately frames him as both scholar and applied weapons specialist. He is not merely a laboratory theorist who studies resonance from a safe distance. His recorded role places him at the point where research, hazardous materials, public defense, and military-grade toolmaking meet. Mortefi's identity is therefore defined by a double prestige: he carries the refinement and early reputation of a New Federation prodigy, while also holding practical authority inside one of Jinzhou's central research institutions.]

[The source identifies him as an international research fellow, a detail that makes him stand apart even within the already unusual environment of Huaxu Academy. He is described as the only International Research Fellow at the institution, which explains why he attracts local curiosity. Mortefi is foreign-born, upper-class, highly educated, and visibly marked by elite habits, yet he chose to leave the comfort of privilege in pursuit of knowledge. That choice gives his identity a sharper edge than a simple noble genius profile would suggest. He is proud, exacting, and aristocratic in manner, but he has also crossed national and social boundaries for the sake of scientific work. His title Dragon's Breath captures both his Fusion power and his professional aura: refined at rest, combustible under pressure, and impossible to treat as ordinary.]

## Appearance

[Mortefi is a red-haired man with glasses, a composed researcher's bearing, and a visual style that combines academic polish with hidden volatility. The source describes him wearing a white lab coat over a black-and-red outfit, which places scientific authority over a palette that already hints at flame, heat, and suppressed aggression. His glasses reinforce the image of an erudite investigator, someone who belongs in lecture halls, laboratories, instrument rooms, and technical briefings. Yet the neatness of that appearance is not neutral. It functions like a controlled surface over a body shaped by resonance, anger, and crystalline fire.]

[His Tacet Mark is located on the right side of his chest, surrounded by red crystalline matter that runs down his right arm like dragon scales. This mark is the center of his most distinctive physical transformation. Mortefi usually conceals the formations with bandages and fire-resistant gloves, suggesting both self-discipline and practical caution. The hidden scales make his appearance more layered than a simple scholar's costume: beneath the lab coat and bandages is a dangerous bodily sign of Fusion resonance. When his fury rises, the scales grow and channel flame like a dragon claw, turning a controlled professional silhouette into something closer to a living weapon. His appearance therefore records the constant tension between refinement and eruption.]

## Forte

[Mortefi's associated Forte is Dragon's Breath, documented under Resonance Assessment 2183-G. According to his own reports, indications of his Awakening began to manifest during his youth, while the actual Awakening occurred alongside a surge of intense anger. That origin is critical to understanding the power. Dragon's Breath is not presented as a detached elemental trick, but as a Fusion ability linked to fury, bodily mutation, and the crystallization of resonance into scale-like matter. When activated, crystalline matter in the shape of dragon scales materializes around the Tacet Mark on his right chest. The scales burn intensely as he harnesses his power, visually and physically connecting Mortefi to the dragon imagery of his title.]

[The source states that Mortefi can generate and manipulate fire through focused concentration, although the preserved assessment text cuts off while describing the mechanism. Even with that truncation, the reliable profile is clear. His Forte allows him to turn Fusion resonance into controlled flame, and his pistol use gives that power a precise, weaponized expression. As head of Tacetite Weaponry research, he is also professionally positioned to understand the relationship between Resonator abilities, Tacetite materials, and engineered armaments. Dragon's Breath therefore matters on two levels. It is his personal combat power, expressed through fire, scales, anger, and pistol-based control. It is also an intellectual subject, something a scientist of his caliber can analyze, refine, and potentially translate into safer or more effective weapon designs for others.]

[His resonance stability data complicates the image of perfect control. Mortefi's waveform graph shows zigzag fluctuations, and while the Time Domain pattern is regular, peak values display an increasing tendency. The assessment reports no abnormal waveforms and notes that peak values remain below the Criticality level, but his Resonant Criticality is high and his stability is low. This combination produces a high risk of Overclocking. Mortefi has a known history of Overclocking, with a maximum recorded Overclock rating of Moderate. During Overclocking, the red scale-shaped crystalline matter on his chest expands continuously across a larger area of skin. His Forte is powerful and sophisticated, but it is not harmless. The same dragon-scale manifestation that gives him identity and combat force also marks the danger line where anger, resonance pressure, and bodily transformation can exceed safe limits.]

## Personality

[Mortefi is lofty, erudite, proud, and intensely conscious of talent. Born into the upper class, he carries the manners and expectations of someone raised around refinement, status, and cultivated taste. The source notes that he was once famed as the youngest genius in the New Federation, a reputation that explains both his confidence and the pressure surrounding him. He does not present as modest in the ordinary sense. He knows his own ability, expects others to recognize it, and responds favorably when treated with appropriate courtesy and praise. The record says it is not hard to win his favor: speak nicely and shower him with ample praise. That detail makes him humorous without making him shallow. Mortefi is vain, but his vanity rests on genuine achievement.]

[His personality is also driven by a thirst for knowledge strong enough to pull him away from privilege. The fact that he abandoned a comfortable upper-class life to become an International Research Fellow at Huaxu Academy is one of the most important character details in the record. It reveals that his arrogance coexists with discipline, ambition, and intellectual hunger. He enjoys classical music, exquisite desserts, and afternoon tea, habits that preserve the cultured texture of his New Federation background. At the same time, he accepts the demanding life of a researcher responsible for Tacetite Weaponry inside Jinzhou's Department of Safety. Mortefi's refinement is not laziness. It is a personal standard that follows him into serious work.]

[Anger is the dangerous undercurrent in his temperament. His Awakening was accompanied by intense anger, and his scales respond when fury rises. This does not mean he is merely hot-headed. The more precise picture is of a man who usually chooses control, structure, and elevated taste, while carrying a Forte that answers violently to emotional escalation. His composed look, bandaged arm, fire-resistant glove, and scientific role all suggest a person who manages himself carefully because he understands what could happen if control fails. Mortefi's pride can make him difficult, but his difficulty is inseparable from the same force that makes him brilliant: a refusal to think small, accept mediocrity, or let his talent remain ornamental.]

## History

[Mortefi was born into the upper class of the New Federation and became renowned for exceptional scientific talent at a young age. The source calls him the youngest genius in the New Federation, a phrase that positions his early life inside both social privilege and intellectual celebrity. His youth also contained the first indications of his Resonator nature. Reports state that signs of Awakening began then, before the full event arrived with a surge of intense anger. From that point forward, Mortefi's body and biography became tied to Dragon's Breath, the Fusion Forte that manifests as burning red dragon scales around his right chest and arm.]

[His later history is marked by departure. Rather than remain within the protection and prestige of his original social world, Mortefi abandoned his privileged life and went to Huaxu Academy in Jinzhou as an International Research Fellow. The source emphasizes how much interest he raises among locals because he is the only person holding that international fellow status at the Academy. This move reshaped him from New Federation prodigy into a foreign specialist embedded in Jinzhou's scientific infrastructure. His habits did not disappear, since classical music, fine desserts, afternoon tea, and aristocratic bearing continue to distinguish him. Yet the context changed. In Jinzhou, those traits surround a working researcher responsible for real dangers and real tools, not a young noble collecting reputation from afar.]

[At Huaxu Academy, Mortefi rose to become head of the Branch of Tacetite Weaponry within the Department of Safety. This title shows that his career matured into applied responsibility. Tacetite Weaponry is not an abstract field, and the Department of Safety implies consequences for both researchers and the broader city. Mortefi's known Overclocking history adds further weight to his work. He is a weapons scholar whose own body demonstrates how powerful resonance can become unstable, and he is a Fusion Resonator whose scales physically expand during crisis. His history therefore ties personal danger to professional expertise. The man who designs or studies dangerous weapons is himself a controlled hazard, a scholar who understands risk because it lives under his bandages.]

## Affiliations

[Mortefi's primary affiliations are Jinzhou and Huaxu Academy, with the New Federation remaining his place of origin and an essential context for his upbringing. In Jinzhou, he belongs to Huaxu Academy's Department of Safety as head of the Branch of Tacetite Weaponry. That affiliation gives him institutional authority and places his genius in service of research with defensive and practical applications. Huaxu Academy is not simply where he studies; it is where his status, labor, and responsibility converge.]

[His New Federation origin remains visible through his upper-class bearing, international status, and cultural preferences. He is not absorbed into Jinzhou as an anonymous researcher, because the record repeatedly marks him as foreign, elite, and distinctive. Instead, he functions as a bridge between the New Federation's image of refined genius and Jinzhou's urgent need for advanced technical talent. His affiliation profile is therefore layered: New Federation by origin, Jinzhou by present service, Huaxu Academy by vocation, and the Department of Safety by formal responsibility. Mortefi's place in the world is defined by that crossing of borders. He brings aristocratic polish and volatile Fusion power into a public research institution, where Dragon's Breath becomes not only a personal title, but a source of expertise that Jinzhou can put to use.]
