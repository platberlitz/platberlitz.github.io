Existing comment: Iuno and Carlotta
SELECTIVE

[Iuno views Carlotta with amused surrender to Montelli cunning. She says Carlotta and her family always manage to bring something fresh to Septimont and Ragunna, even to themselves, and before anyone notices those novelties spark whole new trends. This is Iuno speaking as a priestess who understands patterns but also as a young woman who enjoys being caught by them. Carlotta's world is Ragunna's elite commerce, art investment, family influence, and taste-making power. Iuno reads that world not as dry economics, but as a social current that changes what people desire before they can explain why.]

[Her admission that she usually ends up buying whatever it is gives the relationship a light, personal texture. Iuno is the renowned Priestess of the Tetragon Temple, a figure whose prophecies can shape Septimont's civic life, yet Carlotta's novelties still reach her as temptations. That does not make Iuno foolish. It shows that she recognizes another kind of foresight. Carlotta does not read futures through moonlight, but she reads taste, status, appetite, and opportunity. The Montelli family can introduce an object or fashion so precisely timed that it feels almost prophetic in social terms. Iuno laughs because she sees the trick and still wants the thing.]

[What delights her most is that Carlotta always gets what she likes. To someone whose life is constantly interpreted by Temple expectations, being known through preference rather than destiny may feel unexpectedly pleasant. Carlotta's attention is commercial, artistic, and strategic, but it is still attention. Iuno's cry that the Montellis are cunning as ever carries no real resentment. It is affectionate respect for a family that can turn novelty into trend and trend into desire without making the mechanism too visible. The relationship remains Iuno's viewpoint: Carlotta appears not primarily as a secret troubleshooter or Glacio fighter, but as the elegant source of irresistible newness that even a fate-reading priestess cannot help following.]
