## Acolyte of Ragunna

[Phoebe is a religious acolyte from Ragunna, the Italian-inspired region whose culture is deeply intertwined with traditions of faith, artistic devotion, and the complex institutional structures that mediate between individual believers and the concept of Divinity. As a Spectro-element Rectifier user, Phoebe's connection to the light-aligned element reflects her immersion in religious practice and the spiritual disciplines that Ragunna's institutions cultivate. Her status as an acolyte suggests that she is in the process of formal religious training, not yet fully ordained or consecrated, but deeply committed to the path that will lead her toward full participation in Ragunna's religious hierarchy. Her faith is not abstract or merely intellectual; it is lived, practiced, and tested through the daily disciplines of prayer, service, and the cultivation of spiritual sensitivity.]

## The Dual Playstyle: Absolution and Confession

[Phoebe's combat mechanics revolve around a unique dual-playstyle system that mirrors the theological concepts of absolution and confession, the two poles of the sacramental relationship between the individual soul and divine grace. In her Absolution state, she operates as a high burst damage dealer, utilizing powerful heavy attacks and enhanced Resonance skills to deliver devastating Spectro damage in concentrated bursts. This mode reflects the concept of absolution as a moment of transformative release, the lifting of a burden, the breaking of chains, the sudden clarity that comes when guilt is removed and the soul is freed. In her Confession state, she shifts to a more consistent application of Spectro Frazzle, debuffing enemies and setting up synergistic effects that benefit the entire team. This mode reflects the quieter, more methodical work of confession, the ongoing process of acknowledging faults, applying debuffs to spiritual corruption, and maintaining the constant vigilance that keeps sin and entropy at bay.]

## Prayer, Divine Voice, and Spectro Frazzle

[The resources that Phoebe manages in combat, Prayer and Divine Voice, represent the two-directional flow of communication between the human supplicant and the divine. Prayer is the offering sent upward; Divine Voice is the response received. Her ability to balance these resources, converting between them strategically to maximize either burst damage or sustained Frazzle application, reflects the theological understanding that the relationship between human and divine is not static but dynamic, a constant exchange of offering and response that requires discernment, timing, and the wisdom to know when to speak and when to listen. Her optimal gear includes the Luminous Hymn weapon and Eternal Radiance Echo set, both of which amplify her Spectro capabilities and reinforce her thematic connection to light, truth, and the transformative power of genuine spiritual engagement.]
