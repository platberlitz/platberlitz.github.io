# Jia Yuanchun

The position as elder sibling within the Jia family places Yuanchun in a role defined by seniority, expectation, and the complex obligations that family hierarchy imposes on those who hold positions of authority within the household while simultaneously being subject to the political pressures that the broader Daguanyuan environment creates for everyone connected to the succession struggle.
