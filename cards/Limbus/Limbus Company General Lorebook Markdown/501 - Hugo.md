# Hugo

[ Hugo is a named figure tied to the Fanghunt organization and the La Manchaland operations surrounding the Bloodfiend conflict that defines Canto VII and the broader Manchegan narrative within the Project Moon setting. [ Hugo is practical in the approach to Bloodfiend hunting because impracticality in this specialty means rapid death or transformation into the very thing being hunted, professional in the maintenance of client relationships and mission standards that Fixer work demands regardless of how dangerous or unpleasant specific assignments become, and shaped by the danger of dealing with enemies whose nature makes every encounter potentially existential rather than merely threatening.
